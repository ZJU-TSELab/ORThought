[9, 27, 34, 40, 1, 31, 28, 20, 22]

Decision variable:
1. BerthLocation
Integer variable, \( BerthLocation_{i} \) for all \( i \in V \), representing the berth location of vessel \( i \)
2. BerthTime
Integer variable, \( BerthTime_{i} \) for all \( i \in V \), representing the berthing time of vessel \( i \)
3. LocationRelation
Binary variable, \( LocationRelation_{ij} \) for all \( i, j \in V \), representing the vessel \( i \) located to the left-hand side of vessel \( j \) (1 if true, 0 otherwise)
4. TimeRelation
Binary variable, \( TimeRelation_{ij} \) for all \( i, j \in V \), representing the vessel \( i \) berthing before vessel \( j \) (1 if true, 0 otherwise)

Objective:
The total distance between vessels to the yard and the total penalty cost incurred due to delays
min: \sum_{i\in V}[AdditionalTravelCost_{j}\cdot\left|BerthLocation_{i} - BestBerthLocation_{j}\right| + PenaltyCost_{i} \cdot (BerthTime_{i} - ExpectedArrivalTime_{i}, 0)]

Constraint:
1. MaxBerthLocation
x_{i} + VesselLength_{i} \leq BerthLength, \forall i \in V
2. BerthLocationRelation
x_{i} + VesselLength_{i} \leq x_{j} + M \cdot (1 - LocationRelation_{ij}), \forall i, j \in V, i \neq j
3. BerthTimeRelation
BerthTime_{i} + OperationTime_{i} \leq BerthTime_{j} + M \cdot (1 - TimeRelation_{ij}), \forall i, j \in V, i \neq j
4. Relation
LocationRelation_{ij} + LocationRelation_{ji} + TimeRelation_{ij} + TimeRelation_{ji} \geq 1, \forall i, j \in V, i < j
5. BerthTime
BerthTime_{i} \geq ExpectedArrivalTime_{i}, \forall i \in V
6. BerthLocation
BerthLocation_{i} \geq 0, \forall i \in V


Type:
Integer, Linear, Linear
