Set:
1. Grades
The set of orange grades, \( G = \{9, 6\} \)

Parameter:
1. Inventory (kg)
\( Q_9 = 90,000 \), \( Q_6 = 110,000 \)
2. Quality Scores
\( s_9 = 9 \), \( s_6 = 6 \)
3. FP Economic Data
Revenue \( R_{FP} = 2.80 \), Cost \( C_{FP} = 1.20 \)
4. JE Economic Data
Juice Price \( P_{JE} = 3.00 \), Yield \( Y_{JE} = 0.5 \), Cost \( C_{JE} = 0.90 \)
5. Quality Thresholds
\( T_{FP} = 7.5 \), \( T_{JE} = 8.25 \)
6. Operational Limits
\( JE_{max} = 70,000 \), \( FP_{min} = 20,000 \)

Decision variable:
1. Allocation
Continuous variable \( x_{g,p} \geq 0 \) for all \( g \in G, p \in \{FP, JE\} \), representing kg of grade \( g \) oranges allocated to process \( p \)

Objective:
Maximize daily profit
max: \( \sum_{g\in G} [(R_{FP} - C_{FP})x_{g,FP} + (P_{JE} \cdot Y_{JE} - C_{JE})x_{g,JE}] \)

Constraint:
1. Inventory limits
\( x_{9,FP} + x_{9,JE} \leq Q_9 \)
\( x_{6,FP} + x_{6,JE} \leq Q_6 \)
2. Quality requirements
\( \frac{9x_{9,FP} + 6x_{6,FP}}{x_{9,FP} + x_{6,FP}} \geq T_{FP} \)
\( \frac{9x_{9,JE} + 6x_{6,JE}}{x_{9,JE} + x_{6,JE}} \geq T_{JE} \)
3. Operational constraints
\( x_{9,JE} + x_{6,JE} \leq JE_{max} \)
\( x_{9,FP} + x_{6,FP} \geq FP_{min} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{g,p} \geq 0 \)

Type:
Continuous, Linear, Linear
