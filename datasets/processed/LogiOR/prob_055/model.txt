Set:
1. Items
The set of cargo items, \( I = \{A, B, C, D, E\} \)

Parameter:
1. Weight
The weight of each item \( w_i \) where \( i \in I \):
\( w = \{A:400, B:600, C:300, D:500, E:700\} \)
2. BaseProfit
The base profit of each item \( c_i \) where \( i \in I \):
\( c = \{A:120, B:180, C:90, D:150, E:210\} \)
3. Synergy
The synergy profit matrix \( Q_{ij} \) for all \( i,j \in I \) (as shown in the table)
4. Capacity
The truck's maximum weight capacity \( W = 2000 \)

Decision variable:
1. Selection
Binary variable \( x_i \in \{0, 1\} \) for all \( i \in I \), where \( x_i = 1 \) if item \( i \) is selected, and \( x_i = 0 \) otherwise

Objective:
Maximize total profit including base and synergy values
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} \sum_{j \in I} Q_{ij} x_i x_j + \sum_{i \in I} c_i x_i \)

Constraint:
1. Weight capacity limit
\( \sum_{i \in I} w_i x_i \leq W \)

Type:
Binary, Quadratic, Linear
