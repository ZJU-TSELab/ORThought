Set:
1. Products
The set of available products, \( P = \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8\} \)

Parameter:
1. WarehouseCapacity
500
2. Volume
[80, 60, 40, 70, 50, 30, 90, 20]
3. Profit
[1200, 900, 700, 1100, 800, 500, 1300, 300]
4. IncompatiblePairs
{(1,3), (1,5), (2,4), (3,1), (3,6), (4,2), (4,7), (5,1), (6,3), (7,4)}

Decision variable:
1. ProductSelected
Binary variable \( x_p \in \{0, 1\} \) for all \( p \in P \), where \( x_p = 1 \) if product \( p \) is selected for storage, and \( x_p = 0 \) otherwise

Objective:
Maximize total profit from selected products
max: \sum_{p\in P}Profit_p \cdot x_p

Constraint:
1. Total volume of selected products must not exceed warehouse capacity
\sum_{p\in P}Volume_p \cdot x_p \leq WarehouseCapacity
2. Incompatible products cannot be selected together
x_p + x_q \leq 1, \forall (p,q) \in IncompatiblePairs

Type:
Binary, Linear, Linear
ILP
