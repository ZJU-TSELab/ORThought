Set:
1. Farm
The set of farms, \( F = \{1, 2, 3\} \)  # 1: Farm A, 2: Farm B, 3: Farm C
2. Store
The set of stores, \( S = \{1, 2, 3, 4\} \)  # 1: Store 1, 2: Store 2, 3: Store 3, 4: Store 4

Parameter:
1. ProductionCost
[100, 120, 150]
2. YieldCapacity
[500, 400, 600]
3. WaterUsage
[6, 8, 6]
4. FertilizerUsage
[5, 4, 3]
5. Demand
[200, 300, 400, 500]
6. TransportationCost
[[20, 25, 30, 15], 
 [22, 26, 32, 18], 
 [18, 24, 28, 14]]
7. MaxWaterUsageperTon
7
8. MaxFertilizerUsageperTon
4

Decision variable:
1. AmountProduced
Continuous variable, \( AmountProduced[f][s] \forall f \in F, s \in S \), where \( AmountProduced[f][s] \) is the amount of produce shipped from farm \( f \) to store \( s \).

Objective:
1. Minimize the total cost of production and transportation.
min: \sum_{f \in F} ProductionCost[f] * \sum_{s \in S} AmountProduced[f][s] + \sum_{f \in F} \sum_{s \in S} TransportationCost[f][s] * AmountProduced[f][s]

Constraint:
1. Demand constraint.
\sum_{f \in F} AmountProduced[f][s] \geq Demand[s] \forall s \in S
2. Yield capacity constraint.
\sum_{s \in S} AmountProduced[f][s] \leq YieldCapacity[f] \forall f \in F
3. Water usage constraint.
\sum_{f \in F}\sum_{s \in S} WaterUsage[f] * AmountProduced[f][s] \leq MaxWaterUsageperTon * \sum_{s \in S}\sum_{f \in F} AmountProduced[f][s]
4. Fertilizer usage constraint.
\sum_{f \in F}\sum_{s \in S} FertilizerUsage[f] * AmountProduced[f][s] \leq MaxFertilizerUsageperTon * \sum_{s \in S}\sum_{f \in F} AmountProduced[f][s]
5. Non-negativity constraint.
AmountProduced[f][s] \geq 0 \forall f \in F, s \in S

Type:
Continuous, Linear, Linear
