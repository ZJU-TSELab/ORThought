Set:
1. Categories
The set of products categories, \( I = \{1, 2\} \), where 1 represents Basic Product and 2 represents Advanced Product. 

Parameter:
1. Product Production per Raw Material
\( p = [2, 3] \)
2. Labor Hours per Raw Material
\( h^r = 2 \)
3. Labor Hours per Premium Product
\( h^q = [3, 4] \)
4. Conversion Rate
# The number of products not further processed needed to produce one unit of further processed product.
\( p^q = [1, 1] \) 
5. Processing Cost per Premium Product ($/unit)
\( c^q = [5, 6] \)
6. Product Selling Price ($/unit)
\( s = [10, 12] \)
7. Premium Product Selling Price ($/unit)
\( s^q = [20, 25] \)
8. Raw Material Cost ($/unit)
\( c^r = 5 \)
9. Max Labor Hours
\( H_{\max} = 8000 \)
10. Max Raw Material
\( R_{\max} = 3000 \)

Decision variable:
1. RawMaterialPurchased
Integer variable, \( r \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0} \), the amount of raw material purchased in units.
2. ProductProduced
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}, \forall i \in I \), the amount of product without further processing produced in units.
3. PremiumProductProduced
Integer variable, \( y_{i} \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}, \forall i \in I \), the amount of further processed product produced in units.

Objective:
1. Maximize the total profit.
max: \sum_{i \in I} s_{i} x_{i} + \sum_{i \in I} s^q_{i} y_{i} - c^r r - \sum_{i \in I} c^q_{i} y_{i}
Constraint:
1. Labor hours constraint.
h^r r + \sum_{i \in I} h^q_{i} y_{i} \leq H_{\max}
2. Raw material constraint.
r \leq R_{\max}
3. Material balance constraint.
x_{i} + p^q_{i} y_{i} = p_{i} r, \forall i \in I

Type:
Integer, Linear, Linear
ILP
