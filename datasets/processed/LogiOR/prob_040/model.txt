Set:
1. Product
The set of products, \( P = \{1, 2\} \)  # 1: A, 2: B
2. Quarter
The set of quarters, \( Q = \{1, 2, 3, 4\} \)
3. WorkerGroup
The set of worker groups, \( G = \{1, 2, 3, 4\} \) 

Parameter:
1. HandleProductPerWorkerPerQuarter
[60, 40]
2. WorkerCostPerYear
1800
3. InventoryHoldingCost
70
4. Demand
[[800, 600, 700, 900], [500, 400, 600, 300]]

Decision variable:
1. WorkersInGroup
Integer variable, \( WorkersInGroup[g] \forall g \in G \), the number of workers in group \( g \).
2. ProductProduced
Integer variable, \( ProductProduced[p][q] \forall p \in P, q \in Q \), the amount of product \( p \) produced in quarter \( q \).
3. ProductStorage
Integer variable, \( ProductStorage[p][q] \forall p \in P, q \in Q \), the amount of product \( p \) stored at the end of quarter \( q \).
4. WorkersOnDuty
Integer variable, \( WorkersOnDuty[g][p][q] \forall g \in G, p \in P, q \in Q \), the number of workers in group \( g \) on duty for product \( p \) in quarter \( q \).

Objective:
1. Minimize the total cost.
min: \sum_{g \in G} (WorkersInGroup[g] * WorkerCostPerYear) + \sum_{p \in P, q \in Q} (ProductStorage[p][q] * InventoryHoldingCost)

Constraint:
1. Demand constraint.
ProductProduced[p][1] - ProductStorage[p][1] \geq Demand[p][1] \forall p \in P
ProductProduced[p][q] + ProductStorage[p][q-1] - ProductStorage[p][q] \geq Demand[p][q] \forall p \in P, q \in Q/{1}
2. Production handling constraint.
\sum_{g \in G} WorkersOnDuty[g][p][q] * HandleProductPerWorkerPerQuarter[p] \geq ProductProduced[p][q] \forall p \in P, q \in Q
3. Worker group constraint.
\sum_{p \in P} WorkersOnDuty[g][p][q] \leq WorkersInGroup[g] \forall g \in G, q \in Q
4. Worker off-duty constraint.
WorkersOnDuty[g][p][g] = 0 \forall g \in G, p \in P
5. Inventory constraint.
ProductStorage[p][1] = 0 \forall p \in P
ProductStorage[p][4] = 0 \forall p \in P  # End of year inventory must be 0
4. Non-negativity constraint.
ProductProduced[p][q] \geq 0 \forall p \in P, q \in Q
ProductStorage[p][q] \geq 0 \forall p \in P, q \in Q

Type:
Integer, Linear, Linear
ILP
