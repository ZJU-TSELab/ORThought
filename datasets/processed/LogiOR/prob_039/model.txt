Set:
1. Product
The set of products, \( P = \{1, 2\} \)  # 1: Vaccine X, 2: Vaccine Y
2. Quarter
The set of quarters, \( Q = \{1, 2\} \)  # 1: Quarter 1, 2: Quarter 2

Parameter:
1. Demand
[[100, 200], [300, 100]] # Demand for each product in each quarter
2. MaxProductionPerQuarter
[1500, 1500]
3. RawMaterialCost
[300, 450]
4. RawMaterialPerProduct
[4, 3]
5. MaxRawMaterialPurchase
[2500, 2500]
6. InitialInventory
[[200, 300], [0, 0]] # Initial inventory for each product in each quarter
7. StorageCost
[100, 100] # Storage cost per dose at the end of each quarter
8. EfficacyRate
[0.85, 0.95] # Efficacy rate for each product
9. RegulatoryEfficacyRate
0.90

Decision variable:
1. RawMaterialPurchased
Continous variable, \( RawMaterialPurchased[q] \forall q \in Q \), the amount of raw material purchased in quarter \( q \) in liters.
2. ProductProduced
Integer variable, \( ProductProduced[p][q] \forall p \in P, q \in Q \), the amount of product \( p \) produced in quarter \( q \) in doses.
3. ProductStorage
Integer variable, \( ProductStorage[p][q] \forall p \in P, q \in Q \), the amount of product \( p \) stored at the end of quarter \( q \) in doses.

Objective:
1. Minimize the total cost.
min: \sum_{q \in Q} (RawMaterialCost[q] * RawMaterialPurchased[q] + StorageCost[q] * ProductStorage[p][q] \forall p \in P)

Constraint:
1. Demand constraint.
ProductProduced[p][1] + InitialInventory[p] - ProductStorage[p][1] \geq Demand[p][1] \forall p \in P
ProductProduced[p][q] + ProductStorage[p][q-1] - ProductStorage[p][q] \geq Demand[p][q] \forall p \in P, q \in Q/{1}
2. Production capacity constraint.
\sum_{p \in P} ProductProduced[p][q] \leq MaxProductionPerQuarter[q] \forall q \in Q
3. Raw material constraint.
\sum_{p \in P} RawMaterialPerProduct[p] * ProductProduced[p][q] \leq RawMaterialPurchased[q] \forall q \in Q
4. Raw material purchase limit constraint.
RawMaterialPurchased[q] \leq MaxRawMaterialPurchase[q] \forall q \in Q
5. Regulatory efficacy constraint.
\sum_{p \in P} EfficacyRate[p] * ProductProduced[p][q] \geq \sum_{p \in P} ProductProduced[p][q] * RegulatoryEfficacyRate \forall q \in Q
6. Non-negativity constraint.
ProductProduced[p][q] \geq 0 \forall p \in P, q \in Q
ProductStorage[p][q] \geq 0 \forall p \in P, q \in Q
RawMaterialPurchased[q] \geq 0 \forall q \in Q

Type:
Mixed, Linear, Linear
MILP
