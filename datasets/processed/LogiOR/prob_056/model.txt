Set:
1. TimeSlots
The set of departure times in minutes from 6:00 AM to 9:00 AM, \( T = \{StartTime, 1, 2, \dots, EndTime\} \)

Parameter:
1. StartTime
0
2. EndTime
180
3. MinimumDepartureInterval
5
4. MaximumDepartureInterval
15
5. RoundtripTime
40
6. MaximumBuses
8
7. PassengerArrivalRate
30

Decision variable:
1. Departures
Binary variable \( x_t \in \{0, 1\} \) for all \( t \in T \), where \( x_t = 1 \) if there is a departure at time \( t \), and \( x_t = 0 \) otherwise.

Objective:
The total passenger waiting time
min: \sum_{s\in T}\sum_{t\in T,s>t}(s-t)^2\cdot x_t\cdot x_s

Constraint:
1. Minimum Departure Interval: The interval between consecutive departures must be at least 5 minutes
\sum_{i=t}^{t+MinimumDepartureInterval-1} x_t \leq 1, \forall t \in T \text{ such that } t+MinimumDepartureInterval-1 \leq EndTime

2. Maximum Departure Interval: The interval between consecutive departures must be at most 15 minutes
\sum_{s=t+MinimumDepartureInterval}^{t+MaximumDepartureInterval-1} x_s, \forall t \in T \text{ such that } t+MaximumDepartureInterval-1 \leq EndTime

3. Maximum departure times
\sum_{t\in T}x_t \leq (\left\lfloor\frac{EndTime-StartTime}{RoundtripTime}\right\rfloor+1)MaximumBuses

x_{StartTime} = 1
x_{EndTime} = 1


Type:
Binary, Quadratic, Linear
