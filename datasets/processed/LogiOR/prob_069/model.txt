Set:
1. Nodes
The set of all nodes \( N = \{A, B, C, D, E, F, G\} \), where A is source and D is sink
2. Arcs
The set of directed arcs \( A = \{(A,B), (A,C), (B,C), (B,D), (C,E), (C,F), (E,D), (F,D), (G,D)\} \)

Parameter:
1. Capacity
Pipeline capacity limits \( u_{ij} \): 
\( u_{AB} = 15 \), \( u_{AC} = 10 \), \( u_{BC} = 5 \), \( u_{BD} = 10 \), \( u_{CE} = 8 \), \( u_{CF} = 12 \), \( u_{ED} = 7 \), \( u_{FD} = 20 \), \( u_{GD} = 0 \)

Decision variable:
1. Flow
Non-negative variable \( x_{ij} \geq 0 \) representing flow through arc \( (i,j) \in A \)

Objective:
Maximize total flow from source to sink
max: \( x_{AB} + x_{AC} \) 
(Equivalent to maximizing inflow at sink D)

Constraint:
1. Capacity constraints
\( x_{ij} \leq u_{ij}, \forall (i,j) \in A \)
2. Flow conservation
\( \sum_{(i,k) \in A} x_{ik} = \sum_{(k,j) \in A} x_{kj}, \forall k \in N \setminus \{A,D\} \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{ij} \geq 0, \forall (i,j) \in A \)

Type:
Continuous, Linear, Linear
