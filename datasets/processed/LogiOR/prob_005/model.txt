Set:
1. Industries
The set of industries, \( I = \{1, 2, \dots, IndustryNum\} \)
2. Shops
The set of shops, \( S = \{1, 2, \dots, ShopNum\} \)

Parameter:
1. IndustryNum
3
2. ShopNum
5
3. IndustryStorage
[60, 30, 45]
4. ShopDemand
[15, 35, 20, 5, 40]
5. TransportationCost(row: industry, column: shop)
[[4, 9, 2, 6, 5], 
 [2, 6, 1, 7, 9], 
 [2, 4, 9, 8, 3]]

Decision variable:
1. Transportation
Integer variable, \( t_{ij} \geq 0 \) for all \( i \in I, j \in S \), representing the amount of goods transported from industry \( i \) to shop \( j \)

Objective:
The total transportation cost
min: \sum_{i\in I}\sum_{j\in S}TransportationCost_{ij}\cdot t_{ij}

Constraint:
1. Industry storage capacity
\sum_{j\in S}t_{ij} \leq IndustryStorage_i, \forall i \in I
2. Shop demand
\sum_{i\in I}t_{ij} = ShopDemand_j, \forall j \in S

Type:
Integer, linear, Linear
ILP
