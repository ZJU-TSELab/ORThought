Set:
1. VehicleType
The set of vehicle types, \( V = \{1, 2\} \)  # 1: Type X, 2: Type Y
2. Year
The set of years, \( Y = \{1, 2, 3\} \)  # 1: Year 1, 2: Year 2, 3: Year 3

Parameter:
1. AvgCarbonEmissionLimit
40  # in grams per kilometer
2. RevenuePerVehicle
[25000, 20000]  # in dollars
3. OperatingCostPerVehicle
[18000, 16000]  # in dollars
4. CarbonEmissionPerVehicle
[70, 30]  # in grams per kilometer
5. MaxVehiclesPerYear
[[300, 320, 350], [250, 280, 300]] 
6. MaxTotalVehicles
400  # in vehicles

Decision variable:
1. VehiclesDeployed
Integer variable, \( VehiclesDeployed[v][y] \forall v \in V, y \in Y \), the number of vehicles of type \( v \) deployed in year \( y \).

Objective:
1. Maximize the total profit.
max: \sum_{v \in V, y \in Y} (RevenuePerVehicle[v] * VehiclesDeployed[v][y] - OperatingCostPerVehicle[v] * VehiclesDeployed[v][y])

Constraint:
1. Carbon emissions constraint.
\sum_{v \in V} (CarbonEmissionPerVehicle[v] * VehiclesDeployed[v][y]) \leq AvgCarbonEmissionLimit * \sum_{v \in V} VehiclesDeployed[v][y] \forall y \in Y
2. Fleet capacity constraint.
\sum_{v \in V} VehiclesDeployed[v][y] \leq MaxTotalVehicles \forall y \in Y
3. Yearly vehicle limit constraint.
\sum_{v \in V} VehiclesDeployed[v][y] \leq MaxVehiclesPerYear[v][y] \forall v \in V, y \in Y
4. Non-negativity constraint.
VehiclesDeployed[v][y] \geq 0 \forall v \in V, y \in Y

Type:
Integer, Linear, Linear
ILP
