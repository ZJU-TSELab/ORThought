Set:
1. Nodes
The set of all nodes \( V = \{P1, P2, N1, N2, N3, N4, N5, S1, S2, S3\} \)
2. Arcs
The set of directed arcs \( A \), defined by the connections in problem description
3. Commodities
The set of beverage types \( K = \{Cola, Juice, Water\} \)

Parameter:
1. Capacity
\( u_{ij} \): Arc capacity from node i to j (values given in problem description, with N3->N5 capacity corrected to 100)
2. Cost
\( c_{ij} \): Unit transportation cost on arc (i,j).
3. Demand
\( d_j^k \): Demand for commodity k at store j (values given)
4. Supply
\( s_i^k \): Maximum supply capacity of commodity k at production facility i (values given)

Decision variable:
1. Flow
\( x_{ij}^k \geq 0 \): Flow amount of commodity k on arc (i,j)

Objective:
Minimize total transportation cost
min: \( \sum_{k\in K} \sum_{(i,j)\in A} c_{ij} x_{ij}^k \)

Constraint:
1. Flow conservation
   Net outflow from production nodes must be less than or equal to supply capacity.
   \( \sum_{j:(i,j)\in A} x_{ij}^k - \sum_{j:(j,i)\in A} x_{ji}^k \leq s_i^k \) for production nodes
   \( \sum_{j:(i,j)\in A} x_{ij}^k - \sum_{j:(j,i)\in A} x_{ji}^k = -d_i^k \) for store nodes (demand must be met exactly)
   \( \sum_{j:(i,j)\in A} x_{ij}^k - \sum_{j:(j,i)\in A} x_{ji}^k = 0 \) for intermediate nodes
2. Capacity constraint
   \( \sum_{k\in K} x_{ij}^k \leq u_{ij} \) for all (i,j) ∈ A
3. Non-negativity
   \( x_{ij}^k \geq 0 \) for all (i,j) ∈ A, k ∈ K

Type:
Continuous, Linear, Linear
