Set:
1. Stores
   The set of Stores, \( S = \{S1, S2, S3, S4, S5, S6\} \)

Parameter:
1. CustomerTraffic_s  
   [S1:1200, S2:800, S3:1500, S4:900, S5:1100, S6:700]
2. ShelfSpace_s  
   [S1:50, S2:30, S3:60, S4:35, S5:45, S6:25]
3. SimilarityScore_s  
   [S1:0.8, S2:0.6, S3:0.9, S4:0.7, S5:0.75, S6:0.5]
4. DisplayCost  
   Standard:5, Promotional:15
5. SpaceMultiplier  
   1.2 (for promotional display)
6. TotalInventory  
   300
7. Budget  
   120

Decision variable:
1. AllocateUnits_s  
   Integer variable \( u_s \geq 0 \) for all \( s \in S \), units allocated to store s
2. DisplayType_s  
   Binary variable \( d_s \in \{0,1\} \) for all \( s \in S \), where 1=promotional display

Objective:
Maximize total expected product exposure
max: \( \sum_{s\in S} CustomerTraffic_s \times u_s \times SimilarityScore_s \)

Constraint:
1. Inventory limit  
   \( \sum_{s\in S} u_s \leq TotalInventory \)
2. Budget limit  
   \( \sum_{s\in S} (5(1-d_s) + 15d_s) \leq Budget \)
3. Shelf space constraints  
   \( u_s \leq ShelfSpace_s \times (1 + 0.2d_s), \forall s \in S \)
4. Non-negativity  
   \( u_s \geq 0, \forall s \in S \)

Type:
Integer, Linear, Linear  
ILP
