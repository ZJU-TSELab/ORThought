Set:
1. Product
The set of products, \( P = \{Shirt, Short-sleeve, Casual Cloth\} \)

Parameter:
1. LaborPerUnit
[3, 2, 6] # Labor units required per product
2. MaterialPerUnit
[4, 3, 6] # Material units required per product
3. SellingPrice
[120, 80, 180] # Selling price per product
4. VariableCost
[60, 40, 80] # Variable cost per product
5. LaborAvailable
1500 # Total available labor units per week
6. MaterialAvailable
1600 # Total available material units per week
7. FixedCosts
[2000, 1500, 1000] # Weekly fixed costs for each product's equipment

Decision variable:
1. ProductionQuantity
Integer variable, \( x_{p} \) for all \( p \in P \), representing the number of units of product \( p \) to produce.

Objective:
Maximize the total weekly profit.
max: \sum_{p \in P} (SellingPrice[p] - VariableCost[p]) * x_{p} - \sum_{p \in P} FixedCosts[p]

Constraint:
1. Labor constraint
\sum_{p \in P} LaborPerUnit[p] * x_{p} \leq LaborAvailable
2. Material constraint
\sum_{p \in P} MaterialPerUnit[p] * x_{p} \leq MaterialAvailable
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{p} \geq 0, \forall p \in P \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
ILP
