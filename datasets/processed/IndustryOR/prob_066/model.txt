Set:
1. Products
The set of products, \( P = \{I, II\} \).
2. Months
The set of months in the planning horizon, \( T = \{\text{Jul, Aug, Sep, Oct, Nov, Dec}\} \).

Parameter:
1. Demand
[[30000, 30000, 30000, 100000, 100000, 100000], [15000, 15000, 15000, 50000, 50000, 50000]] # Monthly demand for each product.
2. ProductionCost
[[4.50, 4.50, 4.50, 4.50, 4.50, 4.50], [7.00, 7.00, 7.00, 7.00, 7.00, 7.00]] # Monthly production cost per unit for each product.
3. ProductVolume
[0.2, 0.4] # Volume in cubic meters per unit for each product.
4. MaxProductionCapacity
120000 # Maximum combined production capacity per month.
5. FactoryWarehouseCapacity
15000 # Capacity of the factory's own warehouse in cubic meters.
6. WarehouseCost
[1.0, 1.5] # Cost per cubic meter per month for factory and external warehouses.
7. InitialInventory
[0, 0] # Initial inventory for each product at the start of July.

Decision variable:
1. ProductionQty
Integer variable, \( x_{pt} \) for \( p \in P, t \in T \), represents the units of product p produced in month t.
2. FactoryInventory
Integer variable, \( i_{pt} \) for \( p \in P, t \in T \), represents the units of product p stored in the factory warehouse at the end of month t.
3. ExternalInventory
Integer variable, \( e_{pt} \) for \( p \in P, t \in T \), represents the units of product p stored in the external warehouse at the end of month t.

Objective:
Minimize: \( \sum_{p \in P, t \in T} (\text{ProdCost}_{pt} \cdot x_{pt}) + \sum_{p \in P, t \in T} (1.0 \cdot \text{Volume}_p \cdot i_{pt} + 1.5 \cdot \text{Volume}_p \cdot e_{pt}) \)

Constraint:
1. InventoryBalance
For each product \( p \) and month \( t \), the inventory level is balanced: \( i_{p,t-1} + e_{p,t-1} + x_{pt} - \text{Demand}_{pt} = i_{pt} + e_{pt} \).
2. ProductionCapacity
For each month \( t \), the total production cannot exceed the monthly capacity: \( \sum_{p \in P} x_{pt} \le 120000 \).
3. FactoryWarehouseCapacity
For each month \( t \), the total volume of products in the factory warehouse cannot exceed its capacity: \( \sum_{p \in P} \text{Volume}_p \cdot i_{pt} \le 15000 \).

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
