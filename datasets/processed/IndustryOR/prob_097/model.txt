# Model description for prob_097

Set:
1. Products
The set of products, \( P = \{A, B\} \).

Parameter:
1. GrossProfit
[5000, 11000] # Gross profit in yuan per unit for each product.
2. SteelRequired
[6, 12] # Steel in kg required per unit for each product.
3. AluminumRequired
[8, 20] # Aluminum in kg required per unit for each product.
4. LaborRequired
[11, 24] # Labor in hours required per unit for each product.
5. AvailableSteel
200 # Available steel in kg.
6. AvailableAluminum
300 # Available aluminum in kg.
7. AvailableLabor
300 # Available regular labor hours.
8. OvertimeCost
100 # Cost in yuan per hour of overtime labor.

Decision variable:
1. ProductionQty
Integer variable, \( x_p \) for all \( p \in P \), representing the number of units of product \( p \) to produce.
2. OvertimeHours
Continuous variable, \( o \), representing the total overtime hours used.

Objective:
Maximize the total net profit. Net profit is the gross profit from sales minus the cost of overtime labor.
max: \sum_{p \in P} \text{GrossProfit}[p] * x_p - \text{OvertimeCost} * o

Constraint:
1. Steel Availability
The total steel used cannot exceed the available amount.
\sum_{p \in P} \text{SteelRequired}[p] * x_p \leq \text{AvailableSteel}
2. Aluminum Availability
The total aluminum used cannot exceed the available amount.
\sum_{p \in P} \text{AluminumRequired}[p] * x_p \leq \text{AvailableAluminum}
3. Labor Availability
The total labor hours required must be met by regular hours plus any overtime hours.
\sum_{p \in P} \text{LaborRequired}[p] * x_p \leq \text{AvailableLabor} + o
4. Non-negativity
x_p \geq 0, \forall p \in P
o \geq 0

Type:
Mixed-Integer, linear, linear
MILP
