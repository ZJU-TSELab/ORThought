Set:
1. Vehicle
The set of transportation options, \( V = \{Truck, Van, Motorcycle, EV\} \)

Parameter:
1. Pollution
[100, 50, 10, 0] # Pollution units per trip for each vehicle
2. Capacity
[100, 80, 40, 60] # Units of product transported per trip for each vehicle
3. TotalDemand
1800 # Minimum total units of product to transport
4. MaxPollution
2000 # Maximum total pollution units allowed
5. MinTruckTrips
10 # Minimum number of trips for a truck

Decision variable:
1. Trips
Integer variable, \( x_{v} \) for all \( v \in V \), representing the number of trips for each vehicle.
2. UseVehicle
Binary variable, \( y_{v} \) for \( v \in \{Van, EV\} \), indicating if the vehicle is used.

Objective:
Minimize the total pollution.
min: 100 * x_{Truck} + 50 * x_{Van} * y_{Van} + 10 * x_{Motorcycle}

Constraint:
1. Demand satisfaction
100 * x_{Truck} + 80 * x_{Van} * y_{Van} + 40 * x_{Motorcycle} + 60 * x_{EV} * y_{EV} \geq TotalDemand
2. Pollution limit
100 * x_{Truck} + 50 * x_{Van} * y_{Van} + 10 * x_{Motorcycle} \leq MaxPollution
3. Minimum truck trips
\( x_{Truck} \geq MinTruckTrips \)
4. Exclusive vehicle choice
Only one of Van or Electric Vehicle can be used.
\( y_{Van} + y_{EV} \leq 1 \)
5. Non-negativity
\( x_{v} \geq 0, \forall v \in V \)

Type:
Integer, non-linear, non-linear
