Set:
1. Months
The set of months, \( M = \{1, 2, ..., 6\} \)

Parameter:
1. Demand
[20000, 40000, 42000, 35000, 19000, 18500] # Monthly demand forecast
2. RawMaterialCost
90 # Cost per unit
3. LaborHoursPerUnit
5 # Labor hours required per unit
4. SellingPrice
300 # Selling price per unit
5. InitialWorkers
1000 # Number of workers at the start
6. InitialInventory
15000 # Initial product inventory
7. RegularHourlyWage
30 # Wage per hour for regular time
8. OvertimeHourlyWage
40 # Wage per hour for overtime
9. NormalHoursPerMonth
160 # Normal working hours per worker per month (8 hours/day * 20 days/month)
10. MaxOvertimeHoursPerWorker
20 # Maximum overtime hours per worker per month
11. InventoryCost
15 # Cost to hold one unit in inventory for one month
12. StockoutCost
35 # Penalty for each unit of unmet demand
13. OutsourcingCost
200 # Cost to outsource one unit
14. HiringCost
5000 # Cost to hire one worker
15. FiringCost
8000 # Cost to fire one worker
16. MinEndingInventory
10000 # Minimum required inventory at the end of June

Decision variable:
1. Production
Integer variable, \( P_m \), production quantity in month \( m \).
2. Workers
Integer variable, \( W_m \), number of workers in month \( m \).
3. Hired
Integer variable, \( H_m \), workers hired in month \( m \).
4. Fired
Integer variable, \( F_m \), workers fired in month \( m \).
5. OvertimeHours
Integer variable, \( OT_m \), total overtime hours in month \( m \).
6. Inventory
Integer variable, \( I_m \), inventory at the end of month \( m \).
7. Outsourced
Integer variable, \( O_m \), units outsourced in month \( m \).
8. Stockout
Integer variable, \( S_m \), unmet demand (stockout) in month \( m \).

Objective:
Maximize the total net profit.
max: TotalRevenue - TotalCosts

TotalRevenue = \(\sum_{m \in M} SellingPrice \cdot (Demand_m - S_m)\)
TotalCosts = \(\sum_{m \in M} (RawMaterialCost \cdot P_m + RegularHourlyWage \cdot NormalHoursPerMonth \cdot W_m + OvertimeHourlyWage \cdot OT_m + InventoryCost \cdot I_m + OutsourcingCost \cdot O_m + StockoutCost \cdot S_m + HiringCost \cdot H_m + FiringCost \cdot F_m)\)

Constraint:
1. Worker Balance
\(W_m = W_{m-1} + H_m - F_m, \forall m \in M\) (where \(W_0 = InitialWorkers\))
2. Production Capacity
\(P_m \cdot LaborHoursPerUnit \leq NormalHoursPerMonth \cdot W_m + OT_m, \forall m \in M\)
3. Overtime Limit
\(OT_m \leq MaxOvertimeHoursPerWorker \cdot W_m, \forall m \in M\)
4. Inventory Balance
\(I_m = I_{m-1} + P_m + O_m - (Demand_m - S_m), \forall m \in M\) (where \(I_0 = InitialInventory\))
5. Stockout Definition
\(S_m \geq Demand_m - (I_{m-1} + P_m + O_m), \forall m \in M\)
6. Final Inventory Requirement
\(I_6 \geq MinEndingInventory\)
7. Non-negativity
All decision variables \(\geq 0\).

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
ILP
