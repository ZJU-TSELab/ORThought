Set:
1. Product
The set of products, \( P = \{A_1, A_2, A_3\} \)

Parameter:
1. MaxDemand
[5300, 4500, 5400] # Maximum demand for each product
2. SellingPrice
[124, 109, 115] # Selling price per 100kg for each product
3. ProductionCost
[73.30, 52.90, 65.40] # Production cost per 100kg for each product
4. ProductionQuota
[500, 450, 550] # Maximum production units per day for each product
5. ActivationCost
[170000, 150000, 100000] # Fixed activation cost for each product line
6. MinBatch
[20, 20, 16] # Minimum production batch for each product
7. AvailableDays
22 # Number of available production days

Decision variable:
1. ProductionQuantity
Integer variable, \( x_{p} \) for all \( p \in P \), representing the quantity (in 100kg units) of product \( p \) to produce.
2. UseProduct
Binary variable, \( y_{p} \) for all \( p \in P \), indicating if product \( p \) is produced.

Objective:
Maximize the total profit.
max: \sum_{p \in P} (SellingPrice[p] - ProductionCost[p]) * x_{p} - \sum_{p \in P} ActivationCost[p] * y_{p}

Constraint:
1. Total production time constraint
\sum_{p \in P} (x_{p} / ProductionQuota[p]) \leq AvailableDays
2. Maximum demand constraint
\( x_{p} \leq MaxDemand[p], \forall p \in P \)
3. Minimum batch size constraint
\( x_{p} \geq MinBatch[p] * y_{p}, \forall p \in P \)
4. Activation link constraint
\( x_{p} \leq MaxDemand[p] * y_{p}, \forall p \in P \)
5. Non-negativity
\( x_{p} \geq 0, \forall p \in P \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
ILP
