Integer variable, \( Back_{p,q} \), backlog of product \( p \) at the end of quarter \( q \).

Objective:
Minimize the total cost of compensation and inventory.
min: \(\sum_{p \in P} \sum_{q \in Q} (BacklogCost_p \cdot Back_{p,q} + InventoryCost \cdot Inv_{p,q})\)

Constraint:
1. Production Capacity
\(\sum_{p \in P} HoursPerUnit_p \cdot Prod_{p,q} \leq ProductionHoursAvailable, \forall q \in Q\)
2. Inventory Balance
\(Inv_{p,q-1} + Prod_{p,q} + Back_{p,q} = Demand_{p,q} + Inv_{p,q} + Back_{p,q-1}, \forall p \in P, q \in Q\)
(Initial inventory and backlog are zero)
3. Production Restriction
\(Prod_{I,2} = 0\)
4. Final Inventory
