Set:
1. PlatformType
The set of streaming platforms, \( I = \{0, 1, 2\} \) # 0 for Pi TV, 1 for Beta Video, 2 for Gamma Live

Parameter:
1. Cost
[1200, 2000, 4000] # Cost for a commercial on each platform
2. Reach
[2000, 5000, 9000] # Expected audience reach for each platform
3. Budget
20000 # Weekly budget for commercials
4. BetaLimit
8 # Maximum number of commercials on Beta Video
5. GammaMaxProportion
1/3 # Maximum proportion of commercials on Gamma Live
6. PiMinProportion
0.20 # Minimum proportion of commercials on Pi TV

Decision variable:
1. NumCommercials
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of commercials to run on each platform.

Objective:
Maximize the total audience reach.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} Reach[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Budget constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} Cost[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq Budget \)
2. Beta Video limit
\( x_{1} \leq BetaLimit \)
3. Gamma Live proportion constraint
\( x_{2} \leq GammaMaxProportion \cdot \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \), which simplifies to \( 2x_{2} \leq x_{0} + x_{1} \)
4. Pi TV proportion constraint
\( x_{0} \geq PiMinProportion \cdot \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \), which simplifies to \( 4x_{0} \geq x_{1} + x_{2} \)
5. Non-negativity and Integer
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \) and integer, for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
ILP
