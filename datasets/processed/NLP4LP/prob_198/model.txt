Set:
1. BrandType
The set of brand types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Regular, 1 for Premium

Parameter:
1. CostPerBag
[20, 35] # Cost per bag for each brand type
2. CalciumPerBag
[4, 12] # Units of calcium in each brand type
3. VitaminPerBag
[7, 10] # Units of vitamin mix in each brand type
4. ProteinPerBag
[10, 16] # Units of protein in each brand type
5. MinCalcium
15 # Minimum required units of calcium
6. MinVitamin
20 # Minimum required units of vitamin mix
7. MinProtein
20 # Minimum required units of protein

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfBags
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of bags of each brand to mix.

Objective:
Minimize the total cost.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} CostPerBag[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Calcium constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} CalciumPerBag[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinCalcium \)
2. Vitamin constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} VitaminPerBag[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinVitamin \)
3. Protein constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} ProteinPerBag[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinProtein \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
