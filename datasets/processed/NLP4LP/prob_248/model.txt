Set:
1. InstallationType
The set of installation types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for escalator, 1 for elevator

Parameter:
1. TransportCapacity
[20, 8] # People transported per minute for each type
2. SpaceUsage
[5, 2] # Units of space taken per installation for each type
3. MinTransportCapacity
400 # Minimum total people to be transported per minute
4. MinElevators
2 # Minimum number of elevators to be used

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfInstallations
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of units of each type to install.

Objective:
Minimize the total units of space taken.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} SpaceUsage[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Transport capacity
\( \sum_{i \in I} TransportCapacity[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinTransportCapacity \)
2. Escalator-Elevator ratio
\( x_{0} \geq 3 \cdot x_{1} \)
3. Minimum elevators
\( x_{1} \geq MinElevators \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
