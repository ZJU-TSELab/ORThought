Set:
1. ProductType
The set of product types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Desk, 1 for Drawer

Parameter:
1. AssemblyTimePerProduct
[40, 30] # Minutes of assembly time required for each product type
2. SandingTimePerProduct
[20, 10] # Minutes of sanding time required for each product type
3. MaxAssemblyTime
4000 # Maximum available assembly time in minutes
4. MaxSandingTime
3500 # Maximum available sanding time in minutes
5. ProfitPerProduct
[100, 90] # Profit per product for each type

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfProducts
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of products of each type to make.

Objective:
Maximize the total profit.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} ProfitPerProduct[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Assembly time constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} AssemblyTimePerProduct[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxAssemblyTime \)
2. Sanding time constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} SandingTimePerProduct[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxSandingTime \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
