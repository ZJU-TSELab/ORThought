Set:
1. CookieType
The set of cookie types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for strawberry, 1 for sugar

Parameter:
1. Profit
[5.5, 12] # Profit for each cookie type
2. MaxDemand
[100, 80] # Maximum daily demand for each cookie type
3. MaxTotalProduction
100 # Maximum total number of cookies that can be made

Decision variable:
1. NumCookies
Continuous variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of each cookie type to make.

Objective:
Maximize the total profit.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} Profit[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Demand constraint for strawberry cookies
\( x_{0} \leq MaxDemand[0] \)
2. Demand constraint for sugar cookies
\( x_{1} \leq MaxDemand[1] \)
3. Total production constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \leq MaxTotalProduction \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Continuous, linear, linear
