Set:
1. MealType
The set of meal types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Salad, 1 for Fruit Bowl

Parameter:
1. VitaminPerMeal
[7, 15] # Units of vitamins per meal for each type
2. FibrePerMeal
[12, 3] # Units of fibre per meal for each type
3. PotassiumPerMeal
[2, 8] # Units of potassium per meal for each type
4. MinVitamin
90 # Minimum units of vitamins required
5. MinFibre
110 # Minimum units of fibre required
6. MaxFruitBowlRatio
0.3 # Maximum ratio of fruit bowls to total meals

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfMeals
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of meals of each type to prepare.

Objective:
Maximize the total potassium intake.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} PotassiumPerMeal[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Vitamin requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} VitaminPerMeal[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinVitamin \)
2. Fibre requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} FibrePerMeal[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinFibre \)
3. Fruit bowl proportion
\( x_{1} \leq MaxFruitBowlRatio \cdot \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
