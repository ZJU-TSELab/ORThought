Set:
1. TransportType
The set of transport types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for kayak, 1 for motorboat

Parameter:
1. TransportCapacity
[4, 5] # People transported per trip for each type
2. TimePerTrip
[5, 3] # Minutes per trip for each type
3. MinPeople
550 # Minimum number of people to be transported
4. MaxMotorboatTrips
25 # Maximum number of motorboat trips
5. MinKayakTripRatio
0.75 # Minimum ratio of kayak trips to total trips

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfTrips
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of trips for each transport type.

Objective:
Minimize the total time needed to transport all the locals.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} TimePerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Total people transported
\( \sum_{i \in I} TransportCapacity[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinPeople \)
2. Maximum motorboat trips
\( x_{1} \leq MaxMotorboatTrips \)
3. Kayak trip ratio
\( x_{0} \geq MinKayakTripRatio \cdot \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
