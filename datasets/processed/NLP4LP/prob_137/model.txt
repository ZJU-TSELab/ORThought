Set:
1. VaccineType
The set of vaccine types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Pill, 1 for Shot

Parameter:
1. TimePerVaccine
[10, 20] # Minutes required to administer each vaccine type
2. MaxTime
10000 # Maximum total minutes the clinic operates
3. MinPills
30 # Minimum number of pill vaccines to be administered
4. MinShotToPillRatio
3 # Minimum ratio of shots to pills

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfVaccines
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of vaccines of each type to administer.

Objective:
Maximize the total number of patients that can be vaccinated.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Time constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} TimePerVaccine[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxTime \)
2. Minimum pills constraint
\( x_{0} \geq MinPills \)
3. Shot to pill ratio constraint
\( x_{1} \geq MinShotToPillRatio \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
