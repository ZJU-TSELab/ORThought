Set:
1. MealType
The set of meal types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for crab cakes, 1 for lobster rolls

Parameter:
1. VitaminA
[5, 8] # Units of vitamin A per meal for each meal type
2. VitaminC
[7, 4] # Units of vitamin C per meal for each meal type
3. UnsaturatedFat
[4, 6] # Units of unsaturated fat per meal for each meal type
4. MinVitaminA
80 # Minimum units of vitamin A required
5. MinVitaminC
100 # Minimum units of vitamin C required
6. MaxLobsterRollPercentage
0.4 # Maximum percentage of lobster rolls

Decision variable:
1. MealCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of meals of type \( i \) to be eaten.

Objective:
Minimize the total unsaturated fat intake.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} UnsaturatedFat[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Vitamin A requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} VitaminA[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinVitaminA \)
2. Vitamin C requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} VitaminC[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinVitaminC \)
3. Lobster roll percentage constraint
\( x_{1} \leq MaxLobsterRollPercentage \cdot (x_{0} + x_{1}) \) which simplifies to \( (1 - MaxLobsterRollPercentage) \cdot x_{1} \leq MaxLobsterRollPercentage \cdot x_{0} \) or \( 0.6 \cdot x_{1} \leq 0.4 \cdot x_{0} \) or \( 3 \cdot x_{1} \leq 2 \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
