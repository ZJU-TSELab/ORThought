Set:
1. ContainerType
The set of container types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Small Packets, 1 for Jugs

Parameter:
1. JamVolumePerUnit
[1000, 1250] # Milliliters of jam per unit for each container type
2. TotalJam
65000 # Total milliliters of jam available
3. MinSmallPackets
35 # Minimum number of sets of small packets to be filled
4. JugToPacketRatio
3 # The number of jugs must be at least three times the number of sets of small packets

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfUnits
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of units of each container type.

Objective:
Maximize the total number of units that can be sold.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Jam volume constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} JamVolumePerUnit[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq TotalJam \)
2. Minimum small packets
\( x_{0} \geq MinSmallPackets \)
3. Jug to packet ratio
\( x_{1} \geq JugToPacketRatio \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
