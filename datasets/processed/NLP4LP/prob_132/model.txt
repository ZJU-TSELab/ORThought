Set:
1. PillType
The set of pill types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Large Pill, 1 for Small Pill

Parameter:
1. MedicinalIngredientsPerPill
[3, 2] # Units of medicinal ingredients required for each pill type
2. FillerPerPill
[2, 1] # Units of filler required for each pill type
3. MaxMedicinalIngredients
1000 # Maximum units of medicinal ingredients available
4. MinLargePills
100 # Minimum number of large pills to be made
5. MinSmallPillProportion
0.60 # Minimum proportion of total pills that must be small

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfPills
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of pills of each type to be made.

Objective:
Minimize the total amount of filler material needed.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} FillerPerPill[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Medicinal ingredients constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} MedicinalIngredientsPerPill[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxMedicinalIngredients \)
2. Minimum large pills constraint
\( x_{0} \geq MinLargePills \)
3. Small pill proportion constraint
\( x_{1} \geq MinSmallPillProportion \cdot \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
