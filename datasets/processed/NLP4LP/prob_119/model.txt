Set:
1. PainKillerType
The set of pain killer types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Pain Killer 1, 1 for Pain Killer 2

Parameter:
1. LegMedicine
[0.5, 0.7] # Units of medicine delivered to the legs per dose for each pain killer type
2. BackMedicine
[0.8, 0.4] # Units of medicine delivered to the back per dose for each pain killer type
3. SleepMedicine
[0.3, 0.6] # Units of sleeping medicine delivered per dose for each pain killer type
4. MaxSleepMedicine
8 # Maximum units of sleep medicine allowed
5. MinLegMedicine
4 # Minimum units of medicine to be delivered to the legs

Decision variable:
1. PainKillerDoses
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of doses of each pain killer type.

Objective:
Maximize the amount of medicine delivered to the back.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} BackMedicine[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Sleep medicine constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} SleepMedicine[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxSleepMedicine \)
2. Leg medicine constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} LegMedicine[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinLegMedicine \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
