Set:
1. GeneratorType
The set of generator types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Generator A, 1 for Generator B

Parameter:
1. HydrogenProduction
[40, 30] # Cubic meters of hydrogen produced per day by each generator type
2. PollutantProduction
[300, 200] # Units of pollutants produced per day by each generator type
3. MinHydrogen
1000 # Minimum total cubic meters of hydrogen to be produced per day
4. MaxPollution
3000 # Maximum total units of pollutants allowed per day

Decision variable:
1. GeneratorCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of generators of type \( i \) to be bought.

Objective:
Minimize the total number of generators.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Hydrogen production constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} HydrogenProduction[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinHydrogen \)
2. Pollutant production constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} PollutantProduction[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxPollution \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
