Set:
1. LabType
The set of lab types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Lab 1, 1 for Lab 2

Parameter:
1. HeartPillProduction
[20, 30] # Heart medication pills produced per hour by each lab type
2. LungPillProduction
[30, 40] # Lung medication pills produced per hour by each lab type
3. LaborHours
[3, 5] # Worker hours required per session for each lab type
4. MinHeartPills
20000 # Minimum number of heart medication pills to be produced
5. MinLungPills
30000 # Minimum number of lung medication pills to be produced
6. MaxLaborHours
1500 # Maximum worker hours available

Decision variable:
1. LabSessions
Continuous variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of sessions at each lab.

Objective:
Minimize the total time needed.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} LaborHours[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Heart pill production constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} HeartPillProduction[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinHeartPills \)
2. Lung pill production constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} LungPillProduction[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinLungPills \)
3. Labor hours constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} LaborHours[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxLaborHours \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Continuous, linear, linear
