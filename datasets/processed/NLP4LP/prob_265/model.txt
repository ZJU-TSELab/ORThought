Set:
1. SnackType
The set of snack types, I = {0, 1} # 0 for Spinach, 1 for Soybeans

Parameter:
1. Fibre
[100, 80] # Units of fibre per cup for each snack type
2. Iron
[5, 12] # mg of iron per cup for each snack type
3. Calories
[30, 100] # Calories per cup for each snack type
4. MinFibre
12000 # Minimum total units of fibre required
5. MinIron
300 # Minimum total mg of iron required

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfCups
Integer variable, x_{i} for all i in I, representing the number of cups of each snack type.

Objective:
Maximize the total caloric intake.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} Calories[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Fibre Requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} Fibre[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinFibre \)
2. Iron Requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} Iron[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinIron \)
3. Snack Ratio
\( x_{0} > x_{1} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
