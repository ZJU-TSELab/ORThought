Set:
1. DemonstrationType
The set of demonstration types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Demonstration 1, 1 for Demonstration 2

Parameter:
1. MintUsage
[10, 12] # Units of mint used per demonstration for each demonstration type
2. ActiveIngredientUsage
[20, 15] # Units of active ingredient used per demonstration for each demonstration type
3. MintyFoamProduction
[25, 18] # Units of minty foam produced per demonstration for each demonstration type
4. BlackTarProduction
[5, 3] # Units of black tar produced per demonstration for each demonstration type
5. MaxMint
120 # Maximum units of mint available
6. MaxActiveIngredient
100 # Maximum units of active ingredient available
7. MaxBlackTar
50 # Maximum units of black tar allowed

Decision variable:
1. DemonstrationCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of times each demonstration is done.

Objective:
Maximize the total amount of minty foam produced.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} MintyFoamProduction[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Mint constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} MintUsage[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxMint \)
2. Active ingredient constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} ActiveIngredientUsage[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxActiveIngredient \)
3. Black tar constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} BlackTarProduction[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxBlackTar \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
