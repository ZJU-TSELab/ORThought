Set:
1. JarType
The set of jar types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Small Jar, 1 for Large Jar

Parameter:
1. VolumePerJar
[50, 200] # Milliliters of jam each jar type can hold
2. MinTotalVolume
100000 # Minimum total volume of jam to be shipped

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfJars
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of jars of each type to use.

Objective:
Minimize the total number of jars used.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Minimum volume constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} VolumePerJar[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinTotalVolume \)
2. Jar ratio constraint
\( x_{1} \leq x_{0} \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
