Set:
1. TripType
The set of trip types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Truck, 1 for Car

Parameter:
1. PackagesPerTrip
[50, 30] # Number of packages each vehicle type can transport per trip
2. GasPerTrip
[20, 15] # Liters of gas used for each trip type
3. MaxTruckTrips
5 # Maximum number of truck trips
4. MinCarRatio
0.3 # The number of car trips must be at least 30% of the total trips
5. MinTotalPackages
500 # Minimum total number of packages to be transported

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfTrips
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of trips of each type.

Objective:
Minimize the total amount of gas consumed.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} GasPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Maximum truck trips constraint
\( x_{0} \leq MaxTruckTrips \)
2. Minimum packages constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} PackagesPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinTotalPackages \)
3. Trip ratio constraint
\( x_{1} \geq \frac{MinCarRatio}{1 - MinCarRatio} \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
