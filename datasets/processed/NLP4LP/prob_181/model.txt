Set:
1. TripType
The set of trip types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Helicopter, 1 for Bus

Parameter:
1. PatientsPerTrip
[5, 8] # Number of patients each vehicle type can transport per trip
2. TimePerTrip
[1, 3] # Hours taken for each trip type
3. MinPatients
120 # Minimum number of patients to be transported
4. MinHelicopterRatio
0.3 # The number of helicopter trips must be at least 30% of the total trips
5. MaxBusTrips
10 # Maximum number of bus trips

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfTrips
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of trips of each type.

Objective:
Minimize the total time to transport the patients.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} TimePerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Minimum patients constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} PatientsPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinPatients \)
2. Trip ratio constraint
\( x_{0} \geq \frac{MinHelicopterRatio}{1 - MinHelicopterRatio} \cdot x_{1} \)
3. Maximum bus trips constraint
\( x_{1} \leq MaxBusTrips \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
