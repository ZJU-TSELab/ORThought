Set:
1. NutType
The set of nut types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for almonds, 1 for cashews

Parameter:
1. Calories
[200, 300] # Calories per serving for each nut type
2. Protein
[20, 25] # Grams of protein per serving for each nut type
3. Fat
[15, 12] # Grams of fat per serving for each nut type
4. MinCalories
10000 # Minimum calories required
5. MinProtein
800 # Minimum grams of protein required
6. AlmondToCashewRatio
2 # Minimum ratio of almond servings to cashew servings

Decision variable:
1. NutServings
Continuous variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of servings of each nut type.

Objective:
Minimize the total fat intake.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} Fat[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Calorie requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} Calories[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinCalories \)
2. Protein requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} Protein[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinProtein \)
3. Servings ratio constraint
\( x_{0} \geq AlmondToCashewRatio \cdot x_{1} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Continuous, linear, linear
