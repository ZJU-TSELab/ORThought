Set:
1. TransportationType
The set of transportation types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Bike, 1 for Scooter

Parameter:
1. OrdersPerShift
[10, 7] # Number of orders delivered per shift for each transportation type
2. EnergyPerShift
[5, 6] # Units of energy consumed per shift for each transportation type
3. TipsPerShift
[50, 43] # Amount of tips received per shift for each transportation type
4. MaxTotalShifts
40 # Maximum number of shifts available per month
5. MaxEnergy
230 # Total units of energy available
6. MinOrders
320 # Minimum number of orders to be delivered
7. MinScooterShifts
5 # Minimum number of scooter shifts required

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfShifts
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of shifts for each transportation type.

Objective:
Maximize the total tips received.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} TipsPerShift[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Total shifts limit
\( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \leq MaxTotalShifts \)
2. Energy limit
\( \sum_{i \in I} EnergyPerShift[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxEnergy \)
3. Minimum orders
\( \sum_{i \in I} OrdersPerShift[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinOrders \)
4. Minimum scooter shifts
\( x_{1} \geq MinScooterShifts \)
5. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
