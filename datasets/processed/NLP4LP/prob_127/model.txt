Set:
1. MedicineType
The set of medicine types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Medicine A, 1 for Medicine B

Parameter:
1. ImportedMaterial
[30, 40] # Units of imported material required per dose for each medicine type
2. MRNA
[50, 30] # Units of mRNA required per dose for each medicine type
3. PeopleTreated
[12, 8] # Number of people treated per dose for each medicine type
4. MaxImportedMaterial
300 # Maximum units of imported material available
5. MaxMRNA
400 # Maximum units of mRNA available
6. MaxDosesA
5 # Maximum number of doses of medicine A

Decision variable:
1. MedicineDoses
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of doses of each medicine type.

Objective:
Maximize the total number of people that can be treated.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} PeopleTreated[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Imported material constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} ImportedMaterial[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxImportedMaterial \)
2. mRNA constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} MRNA[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxMRNA \)
3. Maximum doses of medicine A constraint
\( x_{0} \leq MaxDosesA \)
4. Doses relationship constraint
\( x_{1} \geq x_{0} + 1 \)
5. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
