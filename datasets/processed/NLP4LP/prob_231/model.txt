Set:
1. MealType
The set of meal types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Salmon, 1 for Eggs

Parameter:
1. CaloriesPerMeal
[300, 200] # Calories per meal for each type
2. ProteinPerMeal
[15, 8] # Grams of protein per meal for each type
3. SodiumPerMeal
[80, 20] # Milligrams of sodium per meal for each type
4. MinCalories
2000 # Minimum total calories required
5. MinProtein
90 # Minimum total grams of protein required
6. MaxEggRatio
0.4 # Maximum ratio of egg meals to total meals

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfMeals
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of meals of each type.

Objective:
Minimize the total sodium intake.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} SodiumPerMeal[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Calorie requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} CaloriesPerMeal[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinCalories \)
2. Protein requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} ProteinPerMeal[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinProtein \)
3. Egg meal proportion
\( x_{1} \leq MaxEggRatio \cdot \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
