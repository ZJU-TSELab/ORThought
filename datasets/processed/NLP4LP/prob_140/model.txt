Set:
1. TransportType
The set of transport types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Ship, 1 for Plane

Parameter:
1. ContainersPerTrip
[40, 20] # Number of containers transported per trip for each transport type
2. FuelPerTrip
[500, 300] # Liters of fuel consumed per trip for each transport type
3. MinContainers
500 # Minimum number of containers to be transported
4. MaxPlaneTrips
10 # Maximum number of plane trips allowed
5. MinShipTripProportion
0.50 # Minimum proportion of total trips that must be by ship

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfTrips
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of trips for each transport type.

Objective:
Minimize the total amount of fuel consumed.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} FuelPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Container requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} ContainersPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinContainers \)
2. Maximum plane trips
\( x_{1} \leq MaxPlaneTrips \)
3. Ship trip proportion
\( x_{0} \geq MinShipTripProportion \cdot \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
