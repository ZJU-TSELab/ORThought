Set:
1. SouvenirType
The set of souvenir types, \( T = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Elephant, 1 for Tiger

Parameter:
1. WoodAvailable
5000 # Total grams of wood available
2. PlasticAvailable
4000 # Total grams of plastic available
3. Profit
[5, 4] # Profit per souvenir type
4. WoodRequired
[50, 40] # Grams of wood required per souvenir type
5. PlasticRequired
[20, 30] # Grams of plastic required per souvenir type

Decision variable:
1. SouvenirCount
Integer variable, \( x_{t} \) for all \( t \in T \), representing the number of souvenirs of type \( t \) to make.

Objective:
Maximize the total profit.
max: \sum_{t \in T} Profit[t] * x_{t}

Constraint:
1. Wood constraint
\sum_{t \in T} WoodRequired[t] * x_{t} \leq WoodAvailable
2. Plastic constraint
\sum_{t \in T} PlasticRequired[t] * x_{t} \leq PlasticAvailable
3. Non-negativity
x_{t} \geq 0, \forall t \in T

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
