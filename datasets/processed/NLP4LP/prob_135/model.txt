Set:
1. FruitType
The set of fruit types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Blueberries, 1 for Strawberries

Parameter:
1. AntioxidantsPerPack
[3, 1] # Units of anti-oxidants per pack for each fruit type
2. MineralsPerPack
[5, 7] # Units of minerals per pack for each fruit type
3. SugarPerPack
[5, 7] # Grams of sugar per pack for each fruit type
4. MinAntioxidants
90 # Minimum units of anti-oxidants required
5. MinMinerals
100 # Minimum units of minerals required
6. MinStrawberryToBlueberryRatio
3 # Minimum ratio of strawberry packs to blueberry packs

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfPacks
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of packs of each fruit to consume.

Objective:
Minimize total sugar intake.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} SugarPerPack[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Anti-oxidant requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} AntioxidantsPerPack[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinAntioxidants \)
2. Mineral requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} MineralsPerPack[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinMinerals \)
3. Strawberry to blueberry ratio
\( x_{1} \geq MinStrawberryToBlueberryRatio \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
