Set:
1. DeliveryMethod
The set of delivery methods, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Cart, 1 for Hand

Parameter:
1. InteractionsPerHour
[70, 85] # Number of customer interactions per hour for each delivery method
2. RefillsPerHour
[5, 20] # Number of refills per hour for each delivery method
3. MinInteractions
4000 # Minimum total customer interactions per hour
4. MinHandDeliveries
3 # Minimum number of servers delivering by hand
5. MinCartRatio
0.7 # Minimum ratio of cart deliveries to total deliveries

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfDeliveries
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of delivery shifts of each method.

Objective:
Minimize the total number of refills per hour.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} RefillsPerHour[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Customer interactions requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} InteractionsPerHour[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinInteractions \)
2. Minimum hand deliveries
\( x_{1} \geq MinHandDeliveries \)
3. Cart delivery proportion
\( x_{0} \geq MinCartRatio \cdot \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
