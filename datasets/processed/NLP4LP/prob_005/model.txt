Set:
1. MediaType
The set of media types, \( T = \{0, 1, 2\} \) # 0 for z-tube, 1 for soorchle, 2 for wassa

Parameter:
1. Budget
10000 # The weekly advertising budget
2. AdCost
[1000, 200, 100] # Cost per advertisement for each media type
3. Viewers
[400000, 5000, 3000] # Viewers attracted by each advertisement
4. MaxSoorchle
15 # Maximum number of advertisements on soorchle
5. MaxWassaRatio
1/3 # At most a third of the total number of advertisements should occur on wassa
6. MinZTubeRatio
0.05 # At least 5% of the total number of advertisements should occur on z-tube

Decision variable:
1. AdCount
Integer variable, \( x_{t} \) for all \( t \in T \), representing the number of advertisements for media type \( t \).

Objective:
Maximize the total audience.
max: \sum_{t \in T} Viewers[t] * x_{t}

Constraint:
1. Budget constraint
\sum_{t \in T} AdCost[t] * x_{t} \leq Budget
2. Soorchle limit
x_{1} \leq MaxSoorchle
3. Wassa ratio
x_{2} \leq MaxWassaRatio * \sum_{t \in T} x_{t}
4. Z-tube ratio
x_{0} \geq MinZTubeRatio * \sum_{t \in T} x_{t}
5. Non-negativity
x_{t} \geq 0, \forall t \in T

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
