Set:
1. TeaType
The set of tea types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Black Milk Tea, 1 for Matcha Milk Tea

Parameter:
1. MilkPerBottle
[600, 525] # Grams of milk required for each tea type
2. HoneyPerBottle
[10, 5] # Grams of honey required for each tea type
3. MaxMilk
30000 # Maximum available milk in grams
4. MaxHoney
500 # Maximum available honey in grams
5. ProfitPerBottle
[7.5, 5] # Profit per bottle for each tea type

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfBottles
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of bottles of each tea type to make.

Objective:
Maximize the total profit.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} ProfitPerBottle[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Milk constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} MilkPerBottle[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxMilk \)
2. Honey constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} HoneyPerBottle[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxHoney \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
