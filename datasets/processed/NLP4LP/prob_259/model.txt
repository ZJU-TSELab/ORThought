Set:
1. MealType
The set of meal types, I = {0, 1} # 0 for Smoothie, 1 for Protein Bar

Parameter:
1. Protein
[2, 7] # Units of protein per meal for each type
2. Calories
[300, 250] # Calories per meal for each type
3. CalorieLimit
2000 # Maximum total calories allowed

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfMeals
Integer variable, x_{i} for all i in I, representing the number of meals of each type to eat.

Objective:
Maximize the total protein intake.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} \text{Protein}[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Calorie Limit
\( \sum_{i \in I} \text{Calories}[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq \text{CalorieLimit} \)
2. Meal Ratio
\( x_{1} \geq 2 \cdot x_{0} \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
