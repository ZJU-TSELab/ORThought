Set:
1. MealType
The set of meal types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Original, 1 for Experimental

Parameter:
1. FoodWastePerMeal
[20, 25] # Units of food waste generated per meal for each type
2. WrappingWastePerMeal
[45, 35] # Units of wrapping waste generated per meal for each type
3. CookTimePerMeal
[10, 15] # Minutes to cook per meal for each type
4. MaxFoodWaste
800 # Maximum allowable units of food waste
5. MaxWrappingWaste
900 # Maximum allowable units of wrapping waste

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfMeals
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of meals of each type to be pushed for.

Objective:
Minimize the total cooking time.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} CookTimePerMeal[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Food waste limit
\( \sum_{i \in I} FoodWastePerMeal[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxFoodWaste \)
2. Wrapping waste limit
\( \sum_{i \in I} WrappingWastePerMeal[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxWrappingWaste \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
