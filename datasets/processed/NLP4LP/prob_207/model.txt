Set:
1. TacoType
The set of taco types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Regular Taco, 1 for Deluxe Taco

Parameter:
1. ProfitPerTaco
[2.50, 3.55] # Profit per taco for each type
2. MaxDemand
[50, 40] # Maximum demand for each taco type
3. MaxTotalTacos
70 # Maximum total number of tacos that can be sold

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfTacos
Continuous variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of tacos of each type to make.

Objective:
Maximize the total profit.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} ProfitPerTaco[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Maximum demand constraint for Regular Tacos
\( x_{0} \leq MaxDemand[0] \)
2. Maximum demand constraint for Deluxe Tacos
\( x_{1} \leq MaxDemand[1] \)
3. Total tacos constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \leq MaxTotalTacos \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Continuous, linear, linear
