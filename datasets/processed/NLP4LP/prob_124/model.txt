Set:
1. IngredientType
The set of ingredient types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for sulfate, 1 for ginger

Parameter:
1. EffectivenessTime
[0.5, 0.75] # Minutes per unit for each ingredient type to be effective
2. MinSulfate
100 # Minimum units of sulfate to be added
3. TotalUnits
400 # Total units of both ingredients
4. SulfateToGingerRatio
2 # Maximum ratio of sulfate to ginger

Decision variable:
1. IngredientUnits
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of units of each ingredient type.

Objective:
Minimize the total amount of time it takes for the mixture to be effective.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} EffectivenessTime[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Minimum sulfate constraint
\( x_{0} \geq MinSulfate \)
2. Total units constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} = TotalUnits \)
3. Sulfate to ginger ratio constraint
\( x_{0} \leq SulfateToGingerRatio \cdot x_{1} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
