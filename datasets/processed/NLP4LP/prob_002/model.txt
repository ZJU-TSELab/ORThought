Set:
1. AdType
The set of advertisement types, \( T = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Radio, 1 for Social Media

Parameter:
1. Budget
250000 # Total advertising budget
2. AdCost
[5000, 9150] # Cost per ad type: $5,000 for Radio, $9,150 for Social Media
3. AdExposure
[60500, 50000] # Expected exposure per ad type
4. MinRadio
15 # Minimum number of radio ads
5. MaxRadio
40 # Maximum number of radio ads
6. MinSocial
35 # Minimum number of social media ads

Decision variable:
1. AdCount
Integer variable, \( x_{t} \) for all \( t \in T \), representing the number of ads of type \( t \) to run.

Objective:
Maximize the total exposure from the ad campaign.
max: \sum_{t \in T} AdExposure[t] * x_{t}

Constraint:
1. Budget constraint
\sum_{t \in T} AdCost[t] * x_{t} \leq Budget
2. Radio ad lower bound
x_{0} \geq MinRadio
3. Radio ad upper bound
x_{0} \leq MaxRadio
4. Social media ad lower bound
x_{1} \geq MinSocial
5. Non-negativity
x_{t} \geq 0, \forall t \in T

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
