Set:
1. ConcertType
The set of concert types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Pop, 1 for R&B

Parameter:
1. AudiencePerConcert
[100, 240] # Number of audience members per concert for each type
2. PracticeDaysPerConcert
[2, 4] # Days of practice required per concert for each type
3. MinAudience
10000 # Minimum total audience members required
4. MaxPracticeDays
180 # Maximum total practice days available
5. MaxRnBRatio
0.4 # Maximum ratio of R&B concerts to total concerts

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfConcerts
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of concerts of each type.

Objective:
Minimize the total number of concerts.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Audience requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} AudiencePerConcert[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinAudience \)
2. Practice days limit
\( \sum_{i \in I} PracticeDaysPerConcert[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxPracticeDays \)
3. R&B concert proportion
\( x_{1} \leq MaxRnBRatio \cdot \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
