Set:
1. TreatmentType
The set of treatment types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for fertilizer, 1 for seeds

Parameter:
1. EffectivenessTime
[0.5, 1.5] # Minutes per unit for each treatment type to be effective
2. MaxTotalUnits
300 # Maximum total units of fertilizer and seeds
3. MinFertilizer
50 # Minimum units of fertilizer to be added
4. FertilizerToSeedRatio
2 # Maximum ratio of fertilizer to seeds

Decision variable:
1. TreatmentUnits
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of units of each treatment type.
2. TotalTime
Continuous variable, \( T \), representing the total time for the lawn to be ready.

Objective:
Minimize the total time it takes for the lawn to be ready.
min: \( T \)

Constraint:
1. Total units constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \leq MaxTotalUnits \)
2. Minimum fertilizer constraint
\( x_{0} \geq MinFertilizer \)
3. Fertilizer to seed ratio constraint
\( x_{0} \leq FertilizerToSeedRatio \cdot x_{1} \)
4. Time constraint
\( T \geq EffectivenessTime[i] \cdot x_{i} \), for all \( i \in I \)
5. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)
\( T \geq 0 \)

Type:
Mixed-integer, linear, linear
MIP
