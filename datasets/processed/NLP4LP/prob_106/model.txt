Set:
1. FoodType
The set of food types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for burgers, 1 for pizza slices

Parameter:
1. Fat
[10, 8] # Units of fat per serving for each food type
2. Calories
[300, 250] # Calories per serving for each food type
3. Cholesterol
[12, 10] # Units of cholesterol per serving for each food type
4. MinFat
130 # Minimum units of fat required
5. MinCalories
3000 # Minimum calories required
6. PizzaToBurgerRatio
2 # Minimum ratio of pizza slices to burgers

Decision variable:
1. FoodCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of servings of each food type.

Objective:
Minimize the total cholesterol intake.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} Cholesterol[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Fat requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} Fat[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinFat \)
2. Calorie requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} Calories[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinCalories \)
3. Pizza to burger ratio constraint
\( x_{1} \geq PizzaToBurgerRatio \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
