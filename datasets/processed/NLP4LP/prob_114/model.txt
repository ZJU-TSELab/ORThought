Set:
1. MedicationType
The set of medication types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for anxiety medication, 1 for anti-depressants

Parameter:
1. EffectivenessTime
[3, 5] # Minutes per unit for each medication type to be effective
2. MinTotalUnits
100 # Minimum total units of medication
3. MinAnxietyUnits
30 # Minimum units of anxiety medication
4. AnxietyToAntidepressantRatio
2 # Maximum ratio of anxiety medication to anti-depressants

Decision variable:
1. MedicationUnits
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of units of each medication type.

Objective:
Minimize the total time it takes for the medication to be effective.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} EffectivenessTime[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Total units constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \geq MinTotalUnits \)
2. Minimum anxiety units constraint
\( x_{0} \geq MinAnxietyUnits \)
3. Ratio constraint
\( x_{0} \leq AnxietyToAntidepressantRatio \cdot x_{1} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
