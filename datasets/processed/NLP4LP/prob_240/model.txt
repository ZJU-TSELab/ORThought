Set:
1. TransportType
The set of transport types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Freight, 1 for Air

Parameter:
1. TonsPerTrip
[5, 3] # Tons of candles transported per trip for each type
2. CostPerTrip
[300, 550] # Cost per trip for each transport type
3. MinTotalTons
200 # Minimum tons of candles to transport
4. MaxBudget
20000 # Maximum budget available for transportation
5. MinAirTonsRatio
0.3 # Minimum ratio of tons transported by air to total tons
6. MinFreightTrips
5 # Minimum number of freight trips

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfTrips
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of trips for each transport type.

Objective:
Minimize the total number of trips.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Total tons transported
\( \sum_{i \in I} TonsPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinTotalTons \)
2. Budget constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} CostPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxBudget \)
3. Air transportation proportion
\( TonsPerTrip[1] \cdot x_{1} \geq MinAirTonsRatio \cdot \sum_{i \in I} TonsPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \)
4. Minimum freight trips
\( x_{0} \geq MinFreightTrips \)
5. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
