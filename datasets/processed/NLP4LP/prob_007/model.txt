Set:
1. FeedType
The set of feed types, \( T = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Feed A, 1 for Feed B

Parameter:
1. Cost
[100, 80] # Cost per kilogram for each feed type
2. MinProtein
30 # Minimum units of protein required
3. MinFat
50 # Minimum units of fat required
4. ProteinContent
[10, 7] # Units of protein per kilogram for each feed type
5. FatContent
[8, 15] # Units of fat per kilogram for each feed type

Decision variable:
1. FeedAmount
Continuous variable, \( x_{t} \) for all \( t \in T \), representing the amount of feed of type \( t \) in kilograms.

Objective:
Minimize the total cost of the feed mixture.
min: \sum_{t \in T} Cost[t] * x_{t}

Constraint:
1. Protein requirement
\sum_{t \in T} ProteinContent[t] * x_{t} \geq MinProtein
2. Fat requirement
\sum_{t \in T} FatContent[t] * x_{t} \geq MinFat
3. Non-negativity
x_{t} \geq 0, \forall t \in T

Type:
Continuous, linear, linear
