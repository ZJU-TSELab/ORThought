Set:
1. VanType
The set of van types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Regular, 1 for Hybrid

Parameter:
1. DeliveryCapacity
[500, 300] # Packages delivered per day by each van type
2. PollutantProduction
[200, 100] # Units of pollutants produced per day by each van type
3. MinDelivery
20000 # Minimum total packages to be delivered per day
4. MaxPollution
7000 # Maximum total units of pollutants allowed per day

Decision variable:
1. VanCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of vans of type \( i \) to be bought.

Objective:
Minimize the total number of vans.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Delivery requirement constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} DeliveryCapacity[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinDelivery \)
2. Pollution limit constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} PollutantProduction[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxPollution \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
