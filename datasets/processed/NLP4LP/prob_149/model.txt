Set:
1. BouquetType
The set of bouquet types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Small Bouquet, 1 for Large Bouquet

Parameter:
1. FlowersPerBouquet
[5, 10] # Number of flowers in each bouquet type
2. MaxSmallBouquets
80 # Maximum number of small bouquets that can be transported
3. MaxLargeBouquets
50 # Maximum number of large bouquets that can be transported
4. MaxTotalBouquets
70 # Maximum total number of bouquets that can be transported
5. MinLargeBouquets
20 # Minimum number of large bouquets to be transported
6. MinSmallToLargeRatio
2 # Minimum ratio of small bouquets to large bouquets

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfBouquets
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of bouquets of each type to transport.

Objective:
Maximize the total number of flowers that reach the stores.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} FlowersPerBouquet[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Maximum small bouquets constraint
\( x_{0} \leq MaxSmallBouquets \)
2. Maximum large bouquets constraint
\( x_{1} \leq MaxLargeBouquets \)
3. Total bouquets constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \leq MaxTotalBouquets \)
4. Minimum large bouquets constraint
\( x_{1} \geq MinLargeBouquets \)
5. Small to large ratio constraint
\( x_{0} \geq MinSmallToLargeRatio \cdot x_{1} \)
6. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
