Set:
1. TripType
The set of trip types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Ferry, 1 for Light Rail

Parameter:
1. BoxesPerTrip
[20, 15] # Number of boxes of corn each trip type can take
2. MinBoxes
500 # Minimum number of boxes of corn to be sent
3. RailToFerryRatio
4 # The number of light rail trips has to be at least 4 times the number of ferry trips

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfTrips
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of trips of each type.

Objective:
Minimize the total number of trips needed.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Minimum boxes constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} BoxesPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinBoxes \)
2. Trip ratio constraint
\( x_{1} \geq RailToFerryRatio \cdot x_{0} \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
