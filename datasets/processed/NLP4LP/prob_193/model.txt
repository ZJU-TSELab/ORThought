Set:
1. FoodType
The set of food types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Milk, 1 for Vegetables

Parameter:
1. CostPerUnit
[1, 2] # Cost per unit for each food type
2. CalciumPerUnit
[40, 15] # Units of calcium in each food type
3. IronPerUnit
[25, 30] # Units of iron in each food type
4. MinCalcium
100 # Minimum required units of calcium
5. MinIron
50 # Minimum required units of iron

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfUnits
Continuous variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the quantity of each food type to consume.

Objective:
Minimize the total cost.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} CostPerUnit[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Calcium constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} CalciumPerUnit[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinCalcium \)
2. Iron constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} IronPerUnit[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinIron \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Continuous, linear, linear
