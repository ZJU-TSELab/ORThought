Set:
1. ProductType
The set of product types, \( P = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Phones, 1 for Laptops

Parameter:
1. TotalSpace
100 # Total available space in sq. feet
2. MaxCost
5000 # Maximum budget in dollars
3. MaxLabor
2000 # Maximum labor hours available
4. RevenuePerSqFoot
[50, 70] # Net revenue per sq. foot for each product type
5. LaborPerHour
[2, 3] # Labor hours required per sq. foot for each product type
6. CostPerSqFoot
[12, 15] # Cost per sq. foot for each product type

Decision variable:
1. SpaceAllocated
Continuous variable, \( x_{p} \) for all \( p \in P \), representing the sq. feet of space allocated to product type \( p \).

Objective:
Maximize the total revenue.
max: \sum_{p \in P} RevenuePerSqFoot[p] * x_{p}

Constraint:
1. Space constraint
\sum_{p \in P} x_{p} \leq TotalSpace
2. Cost constraint
\sum_{p \in P} CostPerSqFoot[p] * x_{p} \leq MaxCost
3. Labor constraint
\sum_{p \in P} LaborPerHour[p] * x_{p} \leq MaxLabor
4. Non-negativity
x_{p} \geq 0, for all \( p \in P \)

Type:
Linear, continuous, linear
