Set:
1. TripType
The set of trip types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Helicopter, 1 for Car

Parameter:
1. FishPerTrip
[30, 20] # Number of fish each vehicle type can transport per trip
2. TimePerTrip
[40, 30] # Minutes taken for each trip type
3. MaxHelicopterTrips
5 # Maximum number of helicopter trips
4. MinCarRatio
0.6 # The number of car trips must be at least 60% of the total trips
5. MinTotalFish
300 # Minimum total number of fish to be transported

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfTrips
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of trips of each type.

Objective:
Minimize the total time needed.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} TimePerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Maximum helicopter trips constraint
\( x_{0} \leq MaxHelicopterTrips \)
2. Minimum fish constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} FishPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinTotalFish \)
3. Trip ratio constraint
\( x_{1} \geq \frac{MinCarRatio}{1 - MinCarRatio} \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
