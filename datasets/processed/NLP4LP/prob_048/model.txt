Set:
1. BubbleTeaType
The set of bubble tea types, \( T = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Mango, 1 for Lychee

Parameter:
1. TeaPerUnit
[8, 6] # Units of tea required per bubble tea of each type
2. MangoJuiceAvailable
2000 # Units of mango juice available
3. LycheeJuiceAvailable
3000 # Units of lychee juice available
4. MangoJuicePerMangoTea
4 # Units of mango juice required per mango bubble tea
5. LycheeJuicePerLycheeTea
6 # Units of lychee juice required per lychee bubble tea
6. MinLycheeRatio
0.4 # Minimum ratio of lychee bubble teas
7. TeaAvailable
3000 # Units of tea available

Decision variable:
1. BubbleTeaCount
Integer variable, \( x_{t} \) for all \( t \in T \), representing the number of bubble teas of type \( t \) to make.

Objective:
Minimize the total amount of tea needed.
min: \sum_{t \in T} TeaPerUnit[t] * x_{t}

Constraint:
1. Mango juice constraint
MangoJuicePerMangoTea * x_{0} \leq MangoJuiceAvailable
2. Lychee juice constraint
LycheeJuicePerLycheeTea * x_{1} \leq LycheeJuiceAvailable
3. Tea availability constraint
\sum_{t \in T} TeaPerUnit[t] * x_{t} \leq TeaAvailable
4. Lychee ratio constraint
x_{1} \geq MinLycheeRatio * (x_{0} + x_{1})
5. Mango preference constraint
x_{0} \geq x_{1} + 1
6. Non-negativity
x_{t} \geq 0, for all \( t \in T \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
