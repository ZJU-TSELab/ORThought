Set:
1. ProductType
The set of product types, \( T = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Bread, 1 for Cookies

Parameter:
1. MaxMachineHours
3000 # Maximum hours per year for each machine
2. Profit
[5, 3] # Profit per product type
3. MixerTime
[1, 0.5] # Mixer time in hours per product type
4. OvenTime
[3, 1] # Oven time in hours per product type

Decision variable:
1. ProductCount
Continuous variable, \( x_{t} \) for all \( t \in T \), representing the quantity of product of type \( t \) to produce.

Objective:
Maximize the total profit.
max: \sum_{t \in T} Profit[t] * x_{t}

Constraint:
1. Mixer time constraint
\sum_{t \in T} MixerTime[t] * x_{t} \leq MaxMachineHours
2. Oven time constraint
\sum_{t \in T} OvenTime[t] * x_{t} \leq MaxMachineHours
3. Non-negativity
x_{t} \geq 0, \forall t \in T

Type:
Continuous, linear, linear
LP
