Set:
1. SupplementType
The set of supplement types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Supplement A, 1 for Supplement B

Parameter:
1. CostPerPill
[2, 3] # Cost per pill for each supplement type
2. IronPerPill
[5, 4] # Units of iron in each supplement type
3. CalciumPerPill
[10, 15] # Units of calcium in each supplement type
4. MinIron
40 # Minimum required units of iron
5. MinCalcium
50 # Minimum required units of calcium

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfPills
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of pills of each supplement type to take.

Objective:
Minimize the total cost.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} CostPerPill[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Iron constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} IronPerPill[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinIron \)
2. Calcium constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} CalciumPerPill[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinCalcium \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
