Set:
1. DrinkType
The set of drink types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for alpha, 1 for omega

Parameter:
1. Protein
[30, 20] # Grams of protein per bottle for each drink type
2. Sugar
[20, 15] # Grams of sugar per bottle for each drink type
3. Calories
[350, 300] # Calories per bottle for each drink type
4. MinProtein
100 # Minimum grams of protein required
5. MinCalories
2000 # Minimum calories required
6. MaxOmegaPercentage
0.35 # Maximum percentage of omega brand drinks

Decision variable:
1. DrinkCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of bottles of drink type \( i \) to be consumed.

Objective:
Minimize the total sugar intake.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} Sugar[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Protein requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} Protein[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinProtein \)
2. Calorie requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} Calories[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinCalories \)
3. Omega percentage constraint
\( x_{1} \leq MaxOmegaPercentage \cdot (x_{0} + x_{1}) \) which simplifies to \( (1 - MaxOmegaPercentage) \cdot x_{1} \leq MaxOmegaPercentage \cdot x_{0} \) or \( 0.65 \cdot x_{1} \leq 0.35 \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
