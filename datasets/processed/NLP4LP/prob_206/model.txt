Set:
1. MixType
The set of mix types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for First Mix, 1 for Second Mix

Parameter:
1. CatPawSnackRatio
[0.20, 0.35] # Percentage of cat paw snacks in each mix type
2. GoldSharkSnackRatio
[0.80, 0.65] # Percentage of gold shark snacks in each mix type
3. MaxCatPawSnacks
20 # Maximum available cat paw snacks in kg
4. MaxGoldSharkSnacks
50 # Maximum available gold shark snacks in kg
5. ProfitPerKg
[12, 15] # Profit per kg for each mix type

Decision variable:
1. AmountOfMix
Continuous variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the kg of each mix to prepare.

Objective:
Maximize the total profit.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} ProfitPerKg[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Cat paw snacks constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} CatPawSnackRatio[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxCatPawSnacks \)
2. Gold shark snacks constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} GoldSharkSnackRatio[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxGoldSharkSnacks \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Continuous, linear, linear
