Set:
1. WorkerType
The set of worker types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Ultrasound Technician, 1 for Graduate Researcher

Parameter:
1. HoursPerShift
[8, 5] # Hours worked per shift for each worker type
2. CostPerShift
[300, 100] # Cost per shift for each worker type
3. MinHours
500 # Minimum hours of ultrasound services required
4. MaxBudget
14000 # Maximum budget available for shifts
5. TechnicianToResearcherRatio
2 # Required ratio of technician shifts to researcher shifts

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfShifts
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of shifts for each worker type.

Objective:
Minimize the total number of workers (shifts).
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Hours requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} HoursPerShift[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinHours \)
2. Budget constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} CostPerShift[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxBudget \)
3. Regulatory constraint
\( x_{0} = TechnicianToResearcherRatio \cdot x_{1} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
