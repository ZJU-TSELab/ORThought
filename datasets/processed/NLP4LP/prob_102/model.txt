Set:
1. VaccineType
The set of vaccine types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for children's, 1 for adult's

Parameter:
1. MRNA
[50, 75] # mg of mRNA per vaccine for each vaccine type
2. FeverSuppressant
[50, 75] # mg of fever suppressant per vaccine for each vaccine type
3. MaxMRNA
20000 # Maximum mg of mRNA available
4. MinChildrenVaccines
50 # Minimum number of children's vaccines to be made
5. MinAdultVaccinePercentage
0.7 # Minimum percentage of adult vaccines

Decision variable:
1. VaccineCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of vaccines of type \( i \) to be made.

Objective:
Minimize the total amount of fever suppressant used.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} FeverSuppressant[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. mRNA constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} MRNA[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxMRNA \)
2. Adult vaccine percentage constraint
\( x_{1} \geq MinAdultVaccinePercentage \cdot (x_{0} + x_{1}) \) which simplifies to \( (1 - MinAdultVaccinePercentage) \cdot x_{1} \geq MinAdultVaccinePercentage \cdot x_{0} \) or \( 0.3 \cdot x_{1} \geq 0.7 \cdot x_{0} \) or \( 3 \cdot x_{1} \geq 7 \cdot x_{0} \)
3. Minimum children's vaccines constraint
\( x_{0} \geq MinChildrenVaccines \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
