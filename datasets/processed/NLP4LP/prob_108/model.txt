Set:
1. ThermometerType
The set of thermometer types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for electronic, 1 for regular

Parameter:
1. TimePerReading
[3, 2] # Minutes per reading for each thermometer type
2. TotalMinutes
15000 # Total minutes the office is open
3. MinRegularPatients
50 # Minimum number of patients to be checked by a regular thermometer
4. ElectronicToRegularRatio
2 # Minimum ratio of electronic thermometer patients to regular thermometer patients

Decision variable:
1. PatientCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of patients checked by each thermometer type.

Objective:
Maximize the total number of patients whose temperature can be taken.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Time constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} TimePerReading[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq TotalMinutes \)
2. Accuracy constraint
\( x_{0} \geq ElectronicToRegularRatio \cdot x_{1} \)
3. Minimum regular patients constraint
\( x_{1} \geq MinRegularPatients \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
