Set:
1. PillType
The set of pill types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Pill 1, 1 for Pill 2

Parameter:
1. PainMedication
[0.2, 0.6] # Units of pain medication provided by each pill type
2. AnxietyMedication
[0.3, 0.2] # Units of anxiety medication provided by each pill type
3. Discharge
[0.3, 0.1] # Units of discharge caused by each pill type
4. MaxPainMedication
6 # Maximum units of pain medication allowed
5. MinAnxietyMedication
3 # Minimum units of anxiety medication required

Decision variable:
1. PillCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of pills of type \( i \) to be given.

Objective:
Minimize the total amount of discharge.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} Discharge[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Pain medication constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} PainMedication[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxPainMedication \)
2. Anxiety medication constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} AnxietyMedication[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinAnxietyMedication \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
