Set:
1. SmoothieType
The set of smoothie types, I = {0, 1} # 0 for Acai Berry, 1 for Banana Chocolate

Parameter:
1. WaterUsage
[3, 4] # Units of water used per smoothie for each type
2. AcaiBerriesPerSmoothie
7 # Units of acai berries used per acai berry smoothie
3. BananaChocolatePerSmoothie
6 # Units of banana chocolate used per banana chocolate smoothie
4. AcaiBerriesAvailable
3500 # Total units of acai berries available
5. BananaChocolateAvailable
3200 # Total units of banana chocolate available
6. MinAcaiRatio
0.35 # Minimum ratio of acai berry smoothies to total smoothies

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfSmoothies
Integer variable, x_{i} for all i in I, representing the number of smoothies of each type to be made.

Objective:
Minimize the total amount of water used.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} \text{WaterUsage}[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Acai Berries Availability
\( \text{AcaiBerriesPerSmoothie} \cdot x_{0} \leq \text{AcaiBerriesAvailable} \)
2. Banana Chocolate Availability
\( \text{BananaChocolatePerSmoothie} \cdot x_{1} \leq \text{BananaChocolateAvailable} \)
3. Popularity
\( x_{1} > x_{0} \)
4. Loyalty
\( x_{0} \geq \text{MinAcaiRatio} \cdot (x_{0} + x_{1}) \)
5. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
