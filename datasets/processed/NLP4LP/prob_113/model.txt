Set:
1. SupplementType
The set of supplement types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for gummies, 1 for pills

Parameter:
1. Magnesium
[3, 2] # Units of magnesium per supplement for each supplement type
2. Zinc
[4, 5] # Units of zinc per supplement for each supplement type
3. MaxMagnesium
200 # Maximum units of magnesium allowed
4. MinPills
10 # Minimum number of pills to be taken
5. GummyToPillRatio
3 # Minimum ratio of gummies to pills

Decision variable:
1. SupplementCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of each supplement type to be taken.

Objective:
Maximize the total zinc intake.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} Zinc[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Magnesium constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} Magnesium[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxMagnesium \)
2. Minimum pills constraint
\( x_{1} \geq MinPills \)
3. Gummy to pill ratio constraint
\( x_{0} \geq GummyToPillRatio \cdot x_{1} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
