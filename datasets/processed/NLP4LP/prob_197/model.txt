Set:
1. SupplementType
The set of supplement types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Supplement A, 1 for Supplement B

Parameter:
1. CostPerServing
[14, 25] # Cost per serving for each supplement type
2. CalciumPerServing
[30, 60] # Grams of Calcium in each supplement type
3. MagnesiumPerServing
[50, 10] # Grams of Magnesium in each supplement type
4. MinCalcium
400 # Minimum required grams of Calcium
5. MinMagnesium
50 # Minimum required grams of Magnesium

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfServings
Continuous variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of servings of each supplement type to consume.

Objective:
Minimize the total daily cost.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} CostPerServing[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Calcium constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} CalciumPerServing[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinCalcium \)
2. Magnesium constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} MagnesiumPerServing[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinMagnesium \)
3. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Continuous, linear, linear
