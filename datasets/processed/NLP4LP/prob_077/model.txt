Set:
1. BottleType
The set of bottle types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Glass, 1 for Plastic

Parameter:
1. BottleVolume
[500, 750] # ml of water in each bottle type
2. MaxWater
250000 # ml of water available
3. MinGlassBottles
20 # Minimum number of glass bottles to be made
4. PlasticToGlassRatio
3 # Minimum ratio of plastic bottles to glass bottles

Decision variable:
1. BottleCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of bottles of type \( i \) to be made.

Objective:
Maximize the total number of bottles.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Water limit constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} BottleVolume[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxWater \)
2. Minimum glass bottles constraint
\( x_{0} \geq MinGlassBottles \)
3. Plastic to glass ratio constraint
\( x_{1} \geq PlasticToGlassRatio \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
