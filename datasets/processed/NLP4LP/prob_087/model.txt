Set:
1. BottleType
The set of bottle types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Vintage, 1 for Regular

Parameter:
1. BottleVolume
[500, 750] # ml of vine in each bottle type
2. MaxVine
100000 # ml of vine available
3. MinVintageBottles
10 # Minimum number of vintage bottles to be made
4. RegularToVintageRatio
4 # Minimum ratio of regular bottles to vintage bottles

Decision variable:
1. BottleCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of bottles of type \( i \) to be made.

Objective:
Maximize the total number of bottles produced.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Vine volume constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} BottleVolume[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxVine \)
2. Minimum vintage bottles constraint
\( x_{0} \geq MinVintageBottles \)
3. Regular to vintage ratio constraint
\( x_{1} \geq RegularToVintageRatio \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
