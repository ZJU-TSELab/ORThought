Set:
1. BottleType
The set of bottle types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Kids, 1 for Adult

Parameter:
1. BottleVolume
[100, 300] # ml of cough syrup in each bottle type
2. MaxSyrup
25000 # ml of cough syrup available
3. MinKidsBottles
50 # Minimum number of kids size bottles to be made
4. AdultToKidsRatio
3 # Minimum ratio of adult size bottles to kids size bottles

Decision variable:
1. BottleCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of bottles of type \( i \) to be made.

Objective:
Maximize the total number of bottles.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Volume constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} BottleVolume[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxSyrup \)
2. Minimum kids bottles constraint
\( x_{0} \geq MinKidsBottles \)
3. Adult to kids ratio constraint
\( x_{1} \geq AdultToKidsRatio \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
