Set:
1. PrinterType
The set of printer types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Premium, 1 for Regular

Parameter:
1. PrintingSpeed
[30, 20] # Pages printed per minute by each printer type
2. InkConsumption
[4, 3] # Units of ink consumed per minute by each printer type
3. MinPrintingSpeed
200 # Minimum total pages to be printed per minute
4. MaxInkConsumption
35 # Maximum total units of ink to be used per minute

Decision variable:
1. PrinterCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of printers of type \( i \) to be bought.

Objective:
Minimize the total number of printers.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Printing speed constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} PrintingSpeed[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinPrintingSpeed \)
2. Ink consumption constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} InkConsumption[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxInkConsumption \)
3. Preference constraint
\( x_{1} \leq x_{0} - 1 \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
