Set:
1. SwabType
The set of swab types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for throat, 1 for nasal

Parameter:
1. TimePerSwab
[5, 3] # Minutes per swab for each swab type
2. TotalMinutes
20000 # Total minutes the clinic is operational
3. MinNasalSwabs
30 # Minimum number of nasal swabs to be administered
4. ThroatToNasalRatio
4 # Minimum ratio of throat swabs to nasal swabs

Decision variable:
1. SwabCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of swabs of each type to be done.

Objective:
Maximize the total number of patients seen.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Time constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} TimePerSwab[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq TotalMinutes \)
2. Minimum nasal swabs constraint
\( x_{1} \geq MinNasalSwabs \)
3. Throat to nasal ratio constraint
\( x_{0} \geq ThroatToNasalRatio \cdot x_{1} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
