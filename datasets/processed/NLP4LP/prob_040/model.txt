Set:
1. DrillType
The set of drill types, \( T = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for High Intensity, 1 for Low Intensity

Parameter:
1. GemsProcessedPerDay
[50, 30] # Gems processed per day by each drill type
2. WaterRequiredPerDay
[50, 20] # Units of water required per day by each drill type
3. MinGemsToProcess
800 # Minimum gems to process per day
4. MaxWaterAvailable
700 # Maximum units of water available per day
5. MaxHighIntensityRatio
0.4 # Maximum ratio of high intensity drills
6. MinLowIntensityDrills
10 # Minimum number of low intensity drills

Decision variable:
1. DrillCount
Integer variable, \( x_{t} \) for all \( t \in T \), representing the number of drills of type \( t \) to use.

Objective:
Minimize the total number of drills needed.
min: \sum_{t \in T} x_{t}

Constraint:
1. Gem processing capacity constraint
\sum_{t \in T} GemsProcessedPerDay[t] * x_{t} \geq MinGemsToProcess
2. Water availability constraint
\sum_{t \in T} WaterRequiredPerDay[t] * x_{t} \leq MaxWaterAvailable
3. High intensity drill proportion limit
x_{0} \leq MaxHighIntensityRatio * \sum_{t \in T} x_{t}
4. Minimum low intensity drills constraint
x_{1} \geq MinLowIntensityDrills
5. Non-negativity
x_{t} \geq 0, for all \( t \in T \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
