Set:
1. DeliveryMethod
The set of delivery methods, I = {0, 1} # 0 for Runners, 1 for Canoers

Parameter:
1. MailCapacity
[3, 10] # Bags of mail each delivery method can carry
2. TimePerDelivery
[4, 2] # Hours required per delivery for each method
3. TimeAvailable
200 # Total hours available for deliveries
4. MinRunners
4 # Minimum number of runners to be used
5. MaxCanoeRatio
0.33 # Maximum ratio of canoe deliveries to total deliveries

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfDeliveries
Integer variable, x_{i} for all i in I, representing the number of deliveries for each method.

Objective:
Maximize the total amount of mail delivered.
max: \( \sum_{i \in I} \text{MailCapacity}[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Time Availability
\( \sum_{i \in I} \text{TimePerDelivery}[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq \text{TimeAvailable} \)
2. Minimum Runners
\( x_{0} \geq \text{MinRunners} \)
3. Canoe Ratio
\( x_{1} \leq \text{MaxCanoeRatio} \cdot (x_{0} + x_{1}) \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
