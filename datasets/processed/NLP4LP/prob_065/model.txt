Set:
1. SandwichType
The set of sandwich types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Light, 1 for Heavy

Parameter:
1. BreadRequirement
[2, 3] # Slices of bread required for each sandwich type
2. CheeseRequirement
[3, 5] # Slices of cheese required for each sandwich type
3. ProductionTime
[10, 15] # Minutes to make each sandwich type
4. MaxBread
300 # Slices of bread available
5. MaxCheese
500 # Slices of cheese available
6. HeavyToLightRatio
3 # Minimum ratio of heavy to light sandwiches

Decision variable:
1. SandwichCount
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of sandwiches of type \( i \) to be made.

Objective:
Minimize the total production time.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} ProductionTime[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Bread constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} BreadRequirement[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxBread \)
2. Cheese constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} CheeseRequirement[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxCheese \)
3. Demand ratio constraint
\( x_{1} \geq HeavyToLightRatio \cdot x_{0} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
