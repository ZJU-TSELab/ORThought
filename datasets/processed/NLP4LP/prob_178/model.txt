Set:
1. TransportMethod
The set of transport methods, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for High-pressure tube trailer, 1 for Liquefied hydrogen tanker

Parameter:
1. VolumePerTrip
[50, 30] # Cubic meters of hydrogen each method can transport per trip
2. CostPerTrip
[500, 200] # Cost for each trip
3. MinVolume
1000 # Minimum volume of hydrogen to be transported
4. MaxBudget
3750 # Maximum available budget

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfTrips
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of trips of each method.

Objective:
Minimize the total number of trips.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Minimum volume constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} VolumePerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinVolume \)
2. Budget constraint
\( \sum_{i \in I} CostPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \leq MaxBudget \)
3. Trip ratio constraint
\( x_{0} < x_{1} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
