Set:
1. TransportType
The set of transport types, \( I = \{0, 1\} \) # 0 for Boat, 1 for Canoe

Parameter:
1. DucksPerTrip
[10, 8] # Number of ducks transported per trip for each transport type
2. TimePerTrip
[20, 40] # Minutes per trip for each transport type
3. MinDucks
300 # Minimum number of ducks to be transported
4. MaxBoatTrips
12 # Maximum number of boat trips allowed
5. MinCanoeTripProportion
0.60 # Minimum proportion of total trips that must be by canoe

Decision variable:
1. NumberOfTrips
Integer variable, \( x_{i} \) for all \( i \in I \), representing the number of trips for each transport type.

Objective:
Minimize the total amount of time needed to transport the ducks.
min: \( \sum_{i \in I} TimePerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \)

Constraint:
1. Duck transport requirement
\( \sum_{i \in I} DucksPerTrip[i] \cdot x_{i} \geq MinDucks \)
2. Maximum boat trips
\( x_{0} \leq MaxBoatTrips \)
3. Canoe trip proportion
\( x_{1} \geq MinCanoeTripProportion \cdot \sum_{i \in I} x_{i} \)
4. Non-negativity
\( x_{i} \geq 0 \), for all \( i \in I \)

Type:
Integer, linear, linear
